# Business Lens

Clarify business outcomes, success metrics, ROI, and stakeholder alignment.

## Output
Creates/updates `business.md` in the epic folder.

## Focus Areas

### Business Outcomes & Value
- What is the primary business problem being solved?
- What is the expected ROI or value delivery?
- How does this align with company/department OKRs?
- What is the opportunity cost of NOT doing this?

### Success Metrics & KPIs
- How will we measure success? (quantifiable metrics)
- What are the leading indicators?
- What are the lagging indicators?
- What is the baseline for comparison?

### Stakeholder Alignment
- Who are the key stakeholders?
- Who has decision authority?
- Who needs to be consulted vs informed?
- What are the competing priorities?

### Risk & Constraints
- What are the business risks?
- What are the compliance/regulatory constraints?
- What are the budget constraints?
- What is the timeline pressure?

### Market & Customer Context
- Who are the target users/customers?
- What is the competitive landscape?
- What are customer pain points?
- What are market timing considerations?
