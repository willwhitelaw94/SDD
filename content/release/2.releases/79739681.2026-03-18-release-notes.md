## TL;DR

- **Budget email with care plan** — You can now send budget plans and care plans together in a single email from the budget page, replacing the old separate workflow.
- **Service search by keywords** — We've updated the Add Service selector on budgets to now support searching across service names, categories, and keyword tags
- **Service Keywords admin page** — Staff with the right permissions can manage keyword tags on service types to improve search discoverability.
- **Budget event log** — A new event log on the budget plan page shows a timeline of actions (emails sent, items added, plan activated) for audit and traceability.
- **Billing hotfixes** — Fixed unknown service type display on the submit invoice table and corrected bill item amount calculation.

### Budget Email and Care Plan in One

Internal staff and coordinators can now send budget plans and care plans together in a single email from the budget page.

**Why this matters**

Previously, sending the care plan required generating it, downloading it and manually attaching it to the email. Combining the budget plan and care plan into one email reduces overhead and ensures recipients receive a complete package in a single action.

**What's changing**

The budget plan page has a redesigned email drawer. Users can choose to send the budget plan only, the care plan only, or both together. The system generates and attaches the selected documents and sends them in one email.

**Who's affected**

Care coordinators and staff who communicate budget and care plan information to clients or stakeholders.

**Action required**

No action required — this is available immediately to users with budget access.

---

### Service Search with Keyword Tags

The Add Service selector on budgets now supports search by service names, categories, and keyword tags.

**Why this matters**

Users previously had to scroll through long dropdowns or match exact service names. Keyword tags (e.g. "falls alarm") let users find relevant services by intent, improving speed and accuracy when building budgets.

**What's changing**

A new Add Service modal replaces the old dropdown. It uses backend-powered search across service names, categories, and tags. Staff can manage keyword tags on service types via a new Service Keywords page under Staff.

**Who's affected**

Care coordinators building budgets, and staff who manage service type metadata (Admin or users with tag management access).

**Action required**

Staff with `manage-tags` permission can add keyword tags to service types to improve search results. No action required for coordinators — search works immediately.

---

### Budget Event Log

Budget plans now have an event log showing a timeline of actions.

**Why this matters**

Audit and traceability: teams can see when emails were sent, items were added, and when plans were activated, without digging through separate systems.

**What's changing**

A new Events button on the budget plan page opens a modal with a chronological list of events (email sent, item added, item updated, plan activated, funding changes, etc.). Events are stored and displayed per budget plan.

**Who's affected**

Care coordinators and managers who need to track budget plan activity.

**Action required**

No action required — this is available immediately to users with budget access.

---

### Billing Accuracy Fixes

Two hotfixes improve billing data display and calculation.

**Why this matters**

Unknown service types on the submit invoice table and incorrect bill item amounts could cause confusion or errors during bill processing.

**What's changing**

The submit invoice table now correctly displays service types instead of "unknown" in edge cases. Bill item amounts are now calculated via the correct action, ensuring consistency with backend logic.

**Who's affected**

Finance and billing staff who process claims and submit invoices.

**Action required**

No action required — fixes are applied automatically.

### Send Budget and Care Plan in One Email

You can now send the budget plan and care plan together in a single email from the budget page.

**Why we built this**

We heard that sending both documents separately was tedious. This lets you attach and send them together in one go.

**What's changed**

The email drawer on the budget plan page has been redesigned. When you click to send an email, you can choose to include the budget plan, the care plan, or both. Select your options and send — the system attaches the right documents and sends them in one email.

**Where to find it**

`Packages → [Client] → Budget (New) → [Budget Plan] → Send Email` (or the email icon in the header)

**How to use it**

1. Open a budget plan
2. Click the email icon or **Send Email**
3. Choose Budget Plan, Care Plan, or both
4. Enter recipient and optional message
5. Click **Send**

---

### Search Services by Keywords

The Add Service selector on budgets now lets you search by keywords, not just exact service names.

**Why we built this**

Finding the right service in a long list was slow. Now you can search for related terms (e.g. "mobility") and find Physiotherapy and similar services without scrolling.

**What's changed**

The old dropdown is replaced with a searchable modal. Type a keyword, service name, or category — results update as you type. Staff can add keyword tags to service types to improve what shows up in search.

**Where to find it**

`Packages → [Client] → Budget (New) → [Budget Plan] → Add Service` (or when editing an existing service)

**How to use it**

1. Click **Add Service** or edit an existing service
2. Type in the search box (e.g. "mobility", "physio", "transport")
3. Select the service from the results
4. Save

---

### Budget Event Log

You can now see a timeline of actions on each budget plan.

**Why we built this**

It's useful to know when emails were sent, items were added, or the plan was activated. The event log gives you that history in one place.

**What's changed**

A new **Events** button on the budget plan page opens a modal with a chronological list of events: emails sent, items added or updated, plan activated, funding changes, and more.

**Where to find it**

`Packages → [Client] → Budget (New) → [Budget Plan] → Events` (button in the header)

**How to use it**

1. Open a budget plan
2. Click **Events**
3. Scroll through the timeline to see past actions

### Billing Fixes (No Steps Needed)

We fixed two issues: the submit invoice table sometimes showed "unknown" for service types, and bill item amounts could be calculated incorrectly. Both are now fixed.

**No steps needed — this works automatically.**

### Budget Email with Care Plan Attachment

Users can now send budget plans and care plans together in a single email from the budget page.

**Why this was changed**

Users requested the ability to send both documents in one email instead of separate steps. The new drawer consolidates this into a single flow.

**What's different**

The email drawer on the budget plan page has been redesigned. Users can select Budget Plan, Care Plan, or both before sending. The old inline component was replaced with an Inertia modal-based drawer.

**Where in the platform**

`Packages → [Client] → Budget (New) → [Budget Plan]` — use the email icon or **Send Email** in the header.

**How to help users**

Walk them to the budget plan, then the Send Email option. If they don't see Budget (New), they may need the `budget-uplift` feature enabled for their organisation.

**Likely Q&A**

> **Q: "I can't see the Send Email button?"**
> A: Confirm they have `view-budget` permission and are on the Budget (New) view. The Budget (New) link may be behind the `budget-uplift` feature flag for some organisations.

> **Q: "The care plan option is greyed out?"**
> A: The care plan must exist for the package. If there's no care plan, only the budget plan can be sent.

### Service Search with Keyword Tags

The Add Service selector on budgets now supports search by keywords, and staff can manage keyword tags on service types.

**Why this was changed**

Users found it difficult to locate services in long dropdowns. Keyword tags (e.g. "mobility") allow search by intent, improving discoverability.

**What's different**

The old `ServiceSelector` dropdown is replaced with `AddServiceModal`, which supports backend-powered search across service names, categories, and tags. A new Staff page allows managing keyword tags on service types.

**Where in the platform**

- Budget: `Packages → [Client] → Budget → [Budget Plan] → Add Service`
- Service Keywords (Admin): `Staff → Service Keywords` (requires `manage-tags` permission)

**How to help users**

For coordinators: direct them to Add Service and suggest typing keywords. For admin staff managing tags: direct them to Staff → Service Keywords.

**Likely Q&A**

> **Q: "I can't find the Service Keywords page?"**
> A: It requires the `manage-tags` permission. Check the user's role — typically Admin or users with tag management access.

> **Q: "Search returns no results?"**
> A: Suggest trying different keywords or the exact service name. If many services lack tags, results may be limited until staff add keywords.

---

### Budget Event Log

Budget plans now have an event log showing a timeline of actions.

**Why this was changed**

Users and managers wanted visibility into when emails were sent, items were added, and plans were activated for audit and traceability.

**What's different**

A new Events modal on the budget plan page displays a chronological list of events (email sent, item added, item updated, plan activated, funding changes, etc.).

**Where in the platform**

`Packages → [Client] → Budget (New) → [Budget Plan] → Events` (button in the header)

**How to help users**

Direct them to the Events button on the budget plan page. Events are read-only — no actions required.

---

### Billing Hotfixes

Two hotfixes: unknown service type on submit invoice table, and bill item amount calculation.

**Why this was changed**

Users reported "unknown" service types on the submit invoice table and inconsistent bill item amounts. These were bugs in display and calculation logic.

**What's different**

The submit invoice table now correctly resolves and displays service types. Bill item amounts are now calculated via `GenerateClaimInvoiceItems` action for consistency.

**Where in the platform**

Submit invoice flow and bill processing screens.

**How to help users**

No user action needed. If they previously saw "unknown" or wrong amounts, it should be resolved. If issues persist, escalate with the specific bill and invoice details.
