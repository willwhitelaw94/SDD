### Bill Transparency тАФ On-Hold and Rejection Reasons Now Visible

Bills that are put on hold or rejected now display the specific reason directly to the Care Coordinator or Supplier who submitted them.

**Why this matters**

Previously, when a bill was held or rejected, Care Coordinators and Suppliers had no visibility into the reason. This created unnecessary support requests, delayed resolution, and eroded trust with external stakeholders. Making reasons visible reduces friction and gives providers the information they need to act immediately.

**What's changing**

A new badge and tooltip now appear on bills for Care Coordinators and Suppliers, surfacing the on-hold or rejection reason without requiring them to contact the billing team. Additionally, when a Care Management bill fails automated validation, the on-hold comment now includes specific validation error details rather than a generic "see logs" message.

**Who's affected**

Care Coordinators and Suppliers who submit bills through the portal. Finance and billing team staff benefit indirectly through reduced inbound queries.

**Action required**

No action required тАФ this is available immediately for all relevant users.

---

### Fee Exemption Tracking on Bills

TC and CC loading fees can now be marked as exempt on applicable bills, with a full audit trail recording who performed the exemption.

**Why this matters**

Fee exemptions were previously applied without a structured audit trail, making it difficult to reconcile exempted fees during billing reviews or audits. This change introduces accountability and transparency into the exemption process.

**What's changing**

A new `loading_fees_exempted_by` field on bills captures the user who authorised each fee exemption. The Bills table now includes exemption filters and columns, making it easier to report on and manage exempted bills.

**Who's affected**

Finance team members and billing processors who manage fee exemptions on bills.

**Action required**

No action required тАФ the new columns and filters are available immediately in the Bills table.

---

### Team Permissions тАФ Critical Governance Fix

A significant fix to the permission system ensures that team-based role permissions are evaluated correctly across all user contexts.

**Why this matters**

The previous behaviour could, in certain scenarios, cause permission checks to fail or produce incorrect results when a user was operating within a team context. This represented a compliance and governance risk. The fix ensures that all role-based access controls behave as intended.

**What's changing**

The underlying permission evaluation logic has been corrected so that global (team-independent) permissions are always properly checked, and duplicate or cross-contaminated permission lookups are prevented. This is an invisible change for users тАФ they will simply experience correct, consistent access control.

**Who's affected**

All organisations using team-based roles and permissions.

**Action required**

No action required тАФ the fix applies automatically. If users previously reported unexpected permission behaviour, they should retry.

---

### Performance Improvements Across the Portal

Significant optimisations to the portal's hot-path database queries, notification loading, and shared data delivery have been implemented.

**Why this matters**

Portal responsiveness directly impacts user productivity and satisfaction. These changes reduce database load, decrease page load times, and ensure the portal scales efficiently as usage grows.

**What's changing**

Notification queries have been rewritten to use more efficient simple pagination, composite database indexes have been added to the notifications and verification tables, and shared portal data is now deferred and memoised rather than eagerly loaded on every request.

**Who's affected**

All portal users benefit from faster page loads and more responsive interactions.

**Action required**

No action required тАФ improvements are automatic.

---

### Supplier Management Improvements

Multiple fixes to supplier verification, portal stage management, and document display improve the accuracy and reliability of supplier onboarding workflows.

**Why this matters**

Incorrect supplier portal stages and missing document visibility were creating confusion during supplier onboarding and compliance reviews. These fixes ensure suppliers are correctly categorised and that all relevant documents are visible to staff.

**What's changing**

- Suppliers who hold NDIS registration or police check documents are now correctly recognised as verified (previously some were not advancing to the correct portal stage).
- Inviting a supplier via the staff portal now correctly sets their stage to "Invited" immediately.
- Business verification documents are now visible for suppliers who have no services in their price list.
- Expired document alerts on the supplier dashboard no longer appear incorrectly when no documents are required.

**Who's affected**

Staff managing supplier onboarding and compliance; suppliers invited via the portal.

**Action required**

No action required тАФ fixes apply automatically.

### Bills Now Show Why They Were Put On Hold

Bills that have been placed on hold or rejected now display the specific reason directly on the bill, so you know exactly what needs to be resolved.

**Why we built this**

We heard from Care Coordinators and Suppliers that finding out why a bill was held was frustrating тАФ it required contacting the billing team and waiting for a response. Now the reason is right there on the bill.

**What's changed**

A new badge appears on bills that have been held or rejected, showing the reason in a tooltip. You no longer need to contact support to find out what happened with a bill.

**Where to find it**

`Bills тЖТ [select a bill] тЖТ Bill Status Badge`

**How to use it**

1. Open any bill that shows an "On Hold" or "Rejected" status
2. Hover over or tap the status badge to see the reason
3. Take action based on the reason shown (e.g. correct the billing details and resubmit)

Notification loading, page transitions, and overall portal responsiveness have been improved.

**Why we built this**

As the portal grows, some pages were taking longer to load than they should. We've made targeted improvements to the most-used queries and shared data loading to keep things snappy.

**What's changed**

Notifications load faster with more efficient queries. Shared page data (like your sidebar and user info) is now loaded more efficiently on every page. You may notice pages feel more responsive, especially after navigating between sections.

**Where to find it**

Everywhere тАФ this is a system-wide improvement.

**How to use it**

No steps needed тАФ this works automatically.

### Fee Exemptions Now Tracked on Bills

If a TC or CC loading fee has been waived on a bill, this is now clearly shown with a record of who authorised it.

**Why we built this**

This helps finance teams and coordinators understand exactly what fees have been applied or waived on each bill, providing a clear audit trail.

**What's changed**

The Bills table now includes columns and filters for fee exemptions. When you view an exempted bill, you can see who made the exemption.

**Where to find it**

`Bills тЖТ Bills table тЖТ Fee Exemption columns`

**How to use it**

1. Navigate to the Bills table
2. Use the new fee exemption filter to view exempted bills
3. The exemption details are shown inline on each bill row

### Bill Items Can't Be Deleted If Linked to Transactions

Attempting to delete a bill item that has associated financial transactions will now show a clear error message instead of silently failing or causing data issues.

**Why we built this**

Deleting a bill item linked to transactions could create data inconsistencies. This protection ensures billing integrity and gives you a clear message about what's preventing the deletion.

**What's changed**

If you try to delete a bill item with linked transactions, you'll see a validation message explaining why it can't be removed.

**Where to find it**

`Bills тЖТ [select a bill] тЖТ Bill Items тЖТ Delete`

**How to use it**

No steps needed for normal use тАФ you'll only see the error if you try to delete a protected bill item.

### On-Hold and Rejection Reasons Now Visible on Bills

Care Coordinators and Suppliers can now see the on-hold or rejection reason directly on their bills.

**Why this was changed**

Support was receiving a high volume of queries from CCs and suppliers asking why their bills were held. Making this information visible at the point of action reduces that volume significantly.

**What's different**

- Previously: Users had to contact support or billing to find out why a bill was held/rejected.
- Now: A badge and tooltip on the bill displays the reason. For Care Management bills, the automated validation error details are included directly in the on-hold comment.

**Where in the platform**

`Bills тЖТ [any bill with On Hold or Rejected status] тЖТ Status Badge tooltip`

**How to help users**

Direct users to look for the status badge on their bill and hover over it to see the reason. If the reason is still unclear, the billing team can review the on-hold comment for detailed validation error information.

**Likely Q&A**

> **Q: "Why is my bill on hold?"**
> A: The reason is now shown directly on the bill. Ask the user to navigate to their bill and hover over the status badge to see the explanation.

> **Q: "I can't see the on-hold reason on my bill?"**
> A: Confirm they are looking at the bill detail view and hovering over the status badge. The tooltip contains the reason.

### Team Permissions Fix тАФ Critical Change

The underlying permission system has been fixed to ensure team-based role checks work correctly.

**Why this was changed**

In some cases, users operating in a team context could experience incorrect permission evaluations тАФ either being denied access they should have, or permission checks producing inconsistent results.

**What's different**

The permission logic has been corrected. Users should now experience consistent, correct access control. If users previously reported "I can't see X even though I should have access", this may have been resolved.

**Where in the platform**

System-wide тАФ affects all permission-gated features.

**How to help users**

If a user reported a permission issue before this release, ask them to log out and log back in, then retry. If the issue persists, escalate to the technical team.

**Likely Q&A**

> **Q: "I couldn't access [feature] before тАФ is that fixed now?"**
> A: Possibly. Ask them to log out and back in and try again. If it was a team permissions issue, it should now work.

> **Q: "My access changed after the update?"**
> A: Access should be correctly reflecting the user's assigned roles. If something seems wrong, check their role assignment and escalate if needed.

### Fee Exemption Columns Now in Bills Table

The Bills table has new columns and filters showing fee exemptions.

**Why this was changed**

Finance teams needed visibility into which bills have had TC or CC loading fees exempted, and who authorised those exemptions.

**What's different**

New columns in the Bills table show fee exemption status and the user who made the exemption. Filters allow filtering bills by exemption status.

**Where in the platform**

`Bills тЖТ Bills table тЖТ Fee Exemption columns and filters`

**How to help users**

Direct users to the Bills table and point them to the new column headers. The filter options are in the standard table filter panel.

**Likely Q&A**

> **Q: "How do I see which bills have fees waived?"**
> A: Navigate to the Bills table and use the new fee exemption filter to show only exempted bills.

### Bill Item Deletion Now Protected

Deleting a bill item linked to financial transactions is now blocked with a validation message.

**Why this was changed**

Deleting bill items with linked transactions could cause data integrity issues. The system now prevents this with a clear error.

**What's different**

Previously, attempting to delete a linked bill item might have failed silently or caused unexpected behaviour. Now the user sees a validation message explaining why deletion is blocked.

**Where in the platform**

`Bills тЖТ [bill detail] тЖТ Bill Items тЖТ Delete button`

**How to help users**

If a user reports they can't delete a bill item, ask them to check if the bill item has any linked transactions. If so, explain that the item is protected for data integrity reasons.

**Likely Q&A**

> **Q: "I can't delete this bill item тАФ why?"**
> A: If the bill item has linked transactions (payments or adjustments), it cannot be deleted. The portal will show a message explaining this.

### Supplier Portal Stage and Verification Fixes

Multiple fixes to supplier onboarding and verification logic.

**Why this was changed**

Staff reported suppliers not advancing to the correct portal stage after being invited, and expired document alerts appearing incorrectly on supplier dashboards.

**What's different**

- Suppliers invited via the staff portal now immediately show as "Invited" (was not always happening before).
- Suppliers with NDIS or police check documents are now correctly marked as verified.
- Expired document alerts no longer appear on supplier dashboards when no documents are actually required.
- Business verification documents now display correctly even for suppliers with an empty price list.

**Where in the platform**

`Staff тЖТ Suppliers тЖТ [supplier profile]` and supplier-facing dashboard.

**How to help users**

If suppliers report incorrect status or missing documents, ask them to refresh. If the issue relates to verification stage, check the supplier's document list in the staff portal.

**Likely Q&A**

> **Q: "I invited a supplier but their stage still shows wrong?"**
> A: This should now update immediately on invitation. Ask them to refresh. If it persists, escalate.

> **Q: "A supplier says their dashboard shows document errors but they don't need documents?"**
> A: This was a bug that has been fixed. Ask them to refresh their dashboard.
