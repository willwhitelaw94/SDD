This folder contains release notes that have been rotated out of [Releases](/release/releases). Once there are more than 10 releases, the oldest ones are moved here.

Archived releases keep their full content — nothing is removed or summarised. They're browsable in the sidebar and searchable like any other page in tc-docs.

---

## How releases end up here

1. A new release is published
2. If there are now more than 10 releases, the oldest one is moved into this folder
3. The archive grows over time — no further rotation happens from here
