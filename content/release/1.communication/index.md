Resources for communicating releases to stakeholders, including timing guides, audience targeting, and channel selection. Individual resources are browsable in the sidebar.

Every release should be communicated through the appropriate channels depending on the audience and impact. Use the [Stakeholder Matrix](/release/communication/stakeholder-matrix) to determine who needs to know and the [Timing Checklist](/release/communication/timing-checklist) to coordinate the rollout.

---

## Communication Channels

| Channel | When to Use |
|---------|-------------|
| **Release Notes** | Every release — published as markdown in tc-docs |
| **Help Center** | User-facing features — Intercom articles |
| **Teams** | Internal notifications and quick updates |
| **Email** | Major features, support briefs, leadership summaries |
| **Training Video** | Complex features requiring walkthrough |
| **PowerPoint** | Stakeholder briefings and executive summaries |
