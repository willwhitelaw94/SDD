## Primary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder  | Channel          | Timing         | Content       |
| ------------ | ---------------- | -------------- | ------------- |
| Product Team | Teams            | Before release | Heads up      |
| Support Team | Email + Training | Before release | Feature brief |
| Staff Users  | In-app           | After release  | Announcement  |
| Leadership   | Email            | After release  | Summary       |

---

## Communication Timing

| When                | What                              | Who |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------- | --- |
| **2-3 days before** | Brief Support team                | PM  |
| **1 day before**    | Internal Teams notification       | PM  |
| **Release day**     | Post in #releases                 | Dev |
| **Same day**        | Publish Help Center               | PM  |
| **Same day**        | User announcement (if applicable) | PM  |
| **Next day**        | Review feedback                   | PM  |
| **Week 1**          | Leadership summary                | PM  |

---

## Channel Selection Guide

| Channel     | When to Use                                                         | Format              |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Teams/Slack | Internal notifications, quick updates, questions and discussions    | Message             |
| Email       | Support team briefs, leadership summaries, formal communications    | Email               |
| In-App      | User-facing announcements, feature introductions, tips and guidance | In-app message      |
| Help Center | User documentation, how-to guides, FAQ updates                      | Help Center article |
