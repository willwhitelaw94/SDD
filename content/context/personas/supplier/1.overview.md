# Supplier

> Third-party service providers who deliver care services to participants on behalf of Trilogy Care.

## Definition

Under the Support at Home single provider model, Trilogy Care (as the registered provider) may engage **third parties** to deliver services. These suppliers provide direct care while Trilogy Care retains responsibility for compliance and quality.

## Types of Suppliers

### Allied Health Professionals
- Physiotherapists
- Occupational therapists
- Speech pathologists
- Podiatrists
- Dietitians
- Psychologists

### Personal Care Workers
- Support workers
- Care assistants
- Disability support workers

### Home Services
- Cleaners
- Gardeners
- Home maintenance contractors
- Handymen

### Specialist Services
- Nursing agencies
- Respite providers
- Transport services
- Meal delivery services

### AT-HM Suppliers
- Assistive technology suppliers
- Home modification contractors
- Equipment providers

## Role Responsibilities

- Deliver services as booked
- Maintain required qualifications and insurance
- Comply with worker screening requirements
- Document service delivery
- Communicate issues to Trilogy Care
- Follow care plan instructions
- Uphold the Aged Care Code of Conduct

## Goals

- Deliver quality services to participants
- Maintain steady referral stream
- Get paid promptly and accurately
- Build relationship with Trilogy Care
- Expand service offerings
- Minimise administrative burden

## Pain Points

- Complex onboarding requirements
- Multiple provider relationships to manage
- Payment delays or disputes
- Changing participant schedules
- Communication gaps with coordinators
- Understanding participant needs without full context
- Compliance documentation requirements

## Needs from TC Portal

### Service Management
- View assigned bookings
- Confirm service delivery
- Report issues or incidents
- Access participant care instructions

### Administration
- Update availability
- Manage service areas
- Submit invoices
- Track payment status

### Onboarding
- Complete registration requirements
- Upload compliance documents
- Set up pricing and services

### Communication
- Contact coordinators easily
- Receive booking notifications
- Update on participant changes

## Compliance Requirements

Under Support at Home, suppliers must meet:

### Worker Screening
- Police certificate (not older than 3 years)
- No disqualifying convictions
- NDIS check (if applicable)

### Qualifications
- Relevant professional registration
- Current insurance
- Required certifications

### Code of Conduct
- Comply with Aged Care Code of Conduct
- Deliver safe, quality care
- Treat participants with dignity

## Payment Arrangements

- Trilogy Care claims from government
- Pays suppliers according to agreement
- Prices must align with Support at Home capped prices (where applicable)
- No direct billing to participants for subsidised services

## Service Delivery Requirements

### Confirmation
- Confirm services delivered
- Document time and activities
- Report any incidents

### Documentation
- Care notes for clinical services
- Delivery receipts for goods
- Completion records for modifications

## Key Workflows

1. **Onboarding** - Registration, compliance, pricing
2. **Booking** - Receive, accept, schedule
3. **Delivery** - Provide service, document
4. **Confirmation** - Verify delivery for payment
5. **Invoicing** - Submit for payment
