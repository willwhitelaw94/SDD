# Bill Processing Officer

> Invoice processing specialists managing the complete supplier bill lifecycle from receipt through payment.

## Definition

Bill Processing Officers (BPOs) handle supplier invoices, matching them to service plans and ensuring accurate funding allocation. They distinguish between planned services (budgeted) and unplanned services (approved but not budgeted), routing each through appropriate workflows.

## Key Distinction

| Service Type | Definition | BPO Authority |
|--------------|------------|---------------|
| **Planned** | In active service plan/budget | Can process directly |
| **Unplanned** | Approved in NOA but not budgeted | Under $300: can process; Over $300: route to Care Partner |

## Role Responsibilities

### Invoice Receipt & Verification
- Review incoming invoices for completeness
- Verify supplier details and invoice reference
- Check for required documentation
- Detect duplicate invoice submissions

### Bill Item Matching
- Match invoice line items to service plan items using: supplier, rate, date, service type
- Accept "close enough" matches within policy tolerances (±5% rate variance)
- Utilize AI-powered matching recommendations
- Document matching decisions and rationale

### Planned Service Processing
- Process bills for planned services within budget directly (delegated authority)
- Verify sufficient funding exists in funding stream
- Ensure service dates fall within planned period
- Confirm rates match published service plan

### Unplanned Service Identification
- Flag bill items that cannot be matched to planned services
- Check NOA (Notice of Assessment) for service approval
- Process minor unplanned services under threshold ($300) directly if within funding
- Place larger unplanned services on hold for Care Partner approval

### Accuracy & Compliance
- Verify rates against published service plan
- Check supplier is authorized for the service type
- Ensure funding stream eligibility rules followed
- Use correct workflow for planned vs. unplanned

## Approval Authority

| Can Approve | Requires Care Partner |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Planned services within budget | Unplanned services over threshold |
| Planned services over budget BUT within funding stream | Services not approved in NOA |
| Unplanned services under $300 within funding | Questionable matches or discrepancies |

## Bill Matching Process

**Matching Levels:**

1. **Exact Match** - All criteria match perfectly, auto-recommend
2. **Close Match** - Most criteria match, rate within ±5% tolerance, BPO reviews and confirms
3. **No Match** - Cannot be matched, flagged as unplanned, routed to Care Partner

## Goals

- Accurate invoice-to-service-plan matching
- Efficient processing within delegated authority
- Proper routing of unplanned services
- Compliance with funding rules

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Bills processed daily | 90 bills/person |
| Processing time | 5 minutes per bill |
| Matching accuracy | Monitored |
| Error/rework rate | Minimize |

## Pain Points

- High volume of invoices to process
- Missing service plan IDs on invoices
- Rate discrepancies requiring investigation
- Unclear distinction between planned/unplanned in UI
- Training time (targeting reduction from 4 months to 2 weeks)
