# Collections Team

> Finance specialists managing direct debit authorisation, fee payer collection, and accounts receivable for voluntary contributions.

## Definition

The Collections Team handles the collection of voluntary contributions from participants and fee payers. They manage direct debit authorisation, process collections through Easy Collect, and integrate with MYOB for accounts receivable.

## Role Responsibilities

### Direct Debit Management
- Process DD authority forms from fee payers
- Run 152 validation checks on each DD form
- Submit validated forms to Easy Collect platform
- Manage 15-30 day bank activation period
- Track DD status across fee payer accounts

### Fee Payer Management
- Identify and set up fee payers for packages
- Verify fee payer details match bank account holder
- Follow up on unreturned DD forms
- Manage fee payer communication
- Handle fee payer queries and issues

### Collection Processing
- Schedule and submit direct debit collections
- Handle failed collection retries and escalation
- Credit voluntary contribution (VC) funding streams on successful collection
- Process ITF (Incoming Trust Fund) payments

### AR Invoice Integration
- Sync collection transactions with MYOB AR invoices
- Reconcile payments against outstanding invoices
- Handle manual reconciliation for unmatched payments
- Generate AR reports

## Key Concepts

| Term | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| **Collection** | Scheduled retrieval of funds from fee payer's bank account |
| **Fee Payer** | Person or entity responsible for voluntary contribution payments |
| **Direct Debit (DD)** | Automated bank withdrawal authorised by fee payer |
| **DD Authority** | Legal authorisation document allowing direct debit |
| **Easy Collect** | Third-party platform handling DD processing |
| **AR Invoice** | Accounts Receivable invoice in MYOB |
| **ITF Payment** | Incoming Trust Fund payment from fee payers |
| **Validation Flag** | Error or warning requiring manual review before DD activation |

## Current Challenges

| Challenge | Impact | Metric |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| High DD form refusal rate | Cash flow delays | 70% refusal rate |
| Incorrect DD status data | Collection failures | 600+ incorrect statuses |
| Long activation delays | Delayed revenue | 15-30 days typical |
| Manual validation overhead | Staff time burden | 30 days annual work |
| Payment preference shift | Lower DD adoption | 70% CC vs 30% DD |

## Goals

- Decrease activation delays
