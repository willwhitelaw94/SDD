# Care Coordinator

> Day-to-day coordination role handling calls, notes, task completion, and direct participant contact.

## Definition

Care Coordinators handle day-to-day participant interactions and frontline coordination activities. They work under the oversight of [Care Partners](../care-partner/), focusing on regular participant contact and operational tasks.

## Key Distinction

| Role | Focus | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **Care Partner** | Case management | Approvals, oversight, budgets |
| **Care Coordinator** | Day-to-day | Calls, notes, task completion |

## Role Responsibilities

### Care Management Activities

Under Support at Home, care management includes:

**Initial Setup**
- Onboarding new participants
- Developing care plans with participants
- Creating individualised budgets
- Establishing service agreements

**Ongoing Management**
- Monthly contact with participants (minimum)
- Coordinating service delivery
- Managing participant budgets
- Liaising with suppliers
- Documenting care notes

**Reviews and Monitoring**
- Annual care plan reviews
- Responding to changes in needs
- Initiating Support Plan Reviews when required
- Monitoring service quality

**Participant Support**
- Advocating for participant needs
- Supporting decision-making
- Connecting with registered supporters
- Managing complaints and feedback

## Goals

- Deliver high-quality, person-centred care
- Achieve participant goals and outcomes
- Maintain wellness and reablement focus
- Ensure compliance with Quality Standards
- Manage budgets effectively
- Build strong participant relationships

## Pain Points

- High caseload volume
- Administrative burden
- Complex regulatory requirements
- System limitations and workarounds
- Coordinating multiple suppliers
- Managing participant expectations vs budget constraints
- Keeping up with policy changes

## Needs from TC Portal

### Efficiency
- Quick access to participant information
- Streamlined workflows for common tasks
- Bulk actions where appropriate
- Automated reminders and alerts

### Visibility
- Dashboard of caseload status
- Budget tracking at-a-glance
- Upcoming reviews and deadlines
- Service delivery status

### Communication
- Easy contact with participants
- Supplier coordination tools
- Internal team collaboration
- Documentation of interactions

### Compliance
- Care plan templates aligned to standards
- Audit trails for decisions
- Reporting for quality outcomes
- Alerts for overdue activities

## Key Workflows

1. **New Participant Intake** - From referral to first service
2. **Care Plan Development** - Goals, services, budget
3. **Monthly Check-in** - Contact, notes, updates
4. **Budget Management** - Monitoring, adjustments
5. **Supplier Coordination** - Booking, confirmation, issues
6. **Annual Review** - Comprehensive assessment

## Metrics They Care About

- Participant satisfaction
- Budget utilisation
- Service delivery compliance
- Goal achievement
- Overdue review count
- Response times
