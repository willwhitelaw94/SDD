# Administrator

> Trilogy Care operations and finance staff managing provider-level functions.

## Definition

Administrators are internal Trilogy Care staff who handle operational, financial, and compliance functions that support service delivery but don't involve direct participant care coordination.

## Types of Administrators

### Operations Team
- Team leaders and managers
- Rostering and scheduling staff
- Quality and compliance officers
- **Supplier onboarding**: Verify compliance (ABN, insurance, worker screening, qualifications)
- **Quality assurance**: Ensure service delivery meets standards

### Finance Team

#### Accounts Payable
- Bill processing and invoice matching
- Payment approval workflows
- Supplier payment processing
- Fraud prevention (EFTsure integration)

#### Accounts Receivable
- Collections processing
- Direct debit management
- Fee payer validation (152 validation checks per DD form)
- AR invoice reconciliation with MYOB

#### Financial Reporting
- Statement generation (monthly compliance requirement)
- Budget utilisation monitoring
- Funding allocation across streams (ON, RC, EL, CU, HC, VC)
- FP&A and commercial business partner functions

### System Administrators
- User management via Nova Admin Console
- Role and permission assignment
- Team management and assignment
- Portal configuration

### HR and Training
- Staff onboarding
- Training coordination
- Performance management

## Role Responsibilities

### Invoice Processing Pipeline

```
Bills uploaded → BPO matches to service plans → Planned services fast-tracked →
Unplanned flagged for approval → Minor unplanned (<$300) processed by BPO
```

- Match invoice line items to service plans
- Process planned services within budget
- Flag unplanned services for Care Partner approval
- Verify documentation and compliance
- Use AI-powered matching recommendations

### Supplier Management
- Onboard new suppliers (registration, verification, agreements)
- Verify compliance documentation
- Manage digital signature agreements
- Monitor price cap compliance
- Process supplier payments

### Collections & Finance
- Process Direct Debit authority forms
- Manage fee payer validation flags
- Reconcile AR invoices
- Handle escalations and follow-ups
- MYOB integration and sync

## Goals

- Efficient operations
- Accurate financial management
- Regulatory compliance
- Support frontline staff

### Strategic Targets (FY26)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Claims processing cycle time | < 5 days (from 18-24 days) |
| Bill processing cost reduction | -30% |
| Email query backlog | -70% |
| Finance FTE redeployment | 20% shifted to value-add analytics |

## Pain Points

### Bill Processing Challenges
- High deletion rate (currently 66%)
- 90 bills/person/day target difficult to maintain
- Manual matching time-consuming
- AI accuracy still improving (80% initial → 99% goal)

### Collections Challenges
- 70% DD form refusal rate
- 152 validation flags per form
- 15-30 day DD activation delays
- Manual follow-up required for failed payments

### Operational Challenges
- Multiple system integrations (MYOB, EFTsure, My Aged Care)
- Compliance documentation burden
- Coordinating between internal teams

## Key Workflows

1. **Bill Processing** - Invoice receipt → matching → approval → payment
2. **Supplier Onboarding** - Register → verify → agreement → activate
3. **Collections** - DD form → validation → activation → reconciliation
4. **User Management** - Create → assign role → assign team → activate
