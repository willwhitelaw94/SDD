# Assessor

> Aged care needs assessors who determine eligibility and classifications for Support at Home.

## Definition

Assessors are healthcare professionals who conduct comprehensive assessments of older Australians to determine their eligibility for government-funded aged care services and their appropriate classification level (1-8) under Support at Home.

## Types of Assessors

### Government Assessors (External to Trilogy)

#### Single Assessment Organisation (SAO)
- Conducts face-to-face assessments
- Determines eligibility and classification
- Creates Support Plan recommendations
- Uses Integrated Assessment Tool (IAT)

#### My Aged Care Screeners
- Initial phone-based screening
- Identifies urgent needs
- Refers to appropriate assessment pathway

#### ACAT/RAS Assessors
- **ACAT** (Aged Care Assessment Team): Comprehensive needs evaluation for higher care needs
- **RAS** (Regional Assessment Service): Entry-level package assessment

### Internal Assessment Team (Trilogy Care)

The Assessment Team at Trilogy Care processes and validates government assessments:

| Role | What they do |
|------|--------------|
| **Assessment Team** | Initial assessments, IAT processing, booking scheduling |
| **Clinical Team** | Validate complex assessments, provide clinical input |
| **POD Leaders** | Oversee assessment completion and follow-up |

## Role Responsibilities

### External Assessors
- Conduct comprehensive needs assessments using validated tools
- Determine classification levels (1-8)
- Identify clinical and non-clinical needs
- Document assessment findings in IAT
- Recommend services and supports
- Create Support Plan drafts

### Internal Assessment Team
- Process IAT downloads from My Aged Care
- Extract recommendations using AI-assisted tools
- Map recommendations to Tier 5 ISO codes for billing
- Validate low-confidence AI extractions
- Coordinate clinical assessments with suppliers (OT, Physio)
- Link assessment data to care plans

## Assessment Tools & Documents

### Government Assessments

| Type | Source | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| **IAT/Support Plan** | My Aged Care | Determines package level and funding |
| **ACAT Assessment** | Aged Care Assessment Team | Comprehensive needs evaluation |
| **RAS Assessment** | Regional Assessment Service | Entry-level package assessment |
| **Reassessment** | My Aged Care | Package level changes, additional needs |

### Clinical Assessments (Supplier-provided)

| Type | Provider | Common Uses |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **OT Assessment** | Occupational Therapist | Equipment, home modifications, functional capacity |
| **Physio Assessment** | Physiotherapist | Mobility, exercise programs, falls prevention |
| **GP Letter** | General Practitioner | Medication reviews, referrals, medical clearance |
| **Nursing Assessment** | Registered Nurse | Wound care, medication management, clinical needs |

### Validated Assessment Tools

| Tool | Measures | Used For |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Katz ADL** | Activities of Daily Living | Functional independence |
| **Lawton IADL** | Instrumental ADLs | Complex task capability |
| **Mini-Mental State (MMSE)** | Cognitive function | Dementia screening |
| **Geriatric Depression Scale (GDS)** | Depression | Mental health screening |
| **Falls Risk Assessment** | Falls likelihood | Falls prevention planning |
| **Malnutrition Screening Tool (MST)** | Nutrition risk | Dietitian referral |

## Interaction with Trilogy Care

### Assessment Data Flow

```
IAT Available → Download from My Aged Care → AI Extraction →
Validation → Tier 5 Mapping → Care Plan Integration
```

### Key Interactions

- **Receiving IAT data**: Trilogy downloads IAT/Support Plans from My Aged Care
- **Processing clinical assessments**: Suppliers upload OT/Physio reports through the portal
- **Support Plan Reviews**: Triggers reassessment requests when circumstances change
- **AI-powered extraction**: System parses assessments for needs, risks, and recommendations

### Data Extracted from IAT

| Field | Maps To |
|-------|---------|
| **Functional needs** | Care plan needs section |
| **Support goals** | Package goals |
| **Identified risks** | Risk profile |
| **Recommended services** | Service plan |
| **Funding level** | Budget allocation |
| **Review date** | Care plan review schedule |

## Goals

- Accurate determination of care needs
- Appropriate classification levels
- Comprehensive documentation
- Timely processing of assessments
- Evidence-based recommendations

## Pain Points

- Complex regulatory requirements
- High documentation burden
- Coordinating with multiple stakeholders
- Waiting for clinical assessments from suppliers
- Low-confidence AI extractions requiring manual review
