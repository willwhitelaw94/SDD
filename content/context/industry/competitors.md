# Competitor Analysis

A product-focused analysis of key players in the Australian aged care and NDIS home care technology space.

---

## Mable

**Website:** [mable.com.au](https://mable.com.au/)

**Founded:** 2014 | **Model:** Marketplace

### What They Do

Mable is an online marketplace connecting individuals seeking disability and aged care support directly with independent support workers. Users take full control of their care journey - choosing who supports them, when, and at what rate.

### Product Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Marketplace Matching** | AI-driven caregiver recommendations based on needs and preferences |
| **Real-time Tracking** | Live service tracking and session management |
| **Payment Processing** | Secure payments with streamlined record keeping |
| **Verification System** | Strict support worker verification with team confirmation |
| **Insurance** | Every session includes insurance coverage |

### Funding Support

- Self-managed or plan-managed NDIS funding
- Home Care Packages (transitioning to Support at Home Nov 2025)

### Scale

- 21,000+ independent support workers
- 13+ million hours of support delivered

### Pricing Model

No set rates - clients negotiate directly with support workers. Lower fees than traditional providers.

### Strengths

- Large worker network
- Consumer choice and control
- Lower costs than traditional providers
- Strong compliance alignment (NDIS, Aged Care frameworks)

### Weaknesses

- Less oversight than fully-managed models
- Consumer must self-manage relationships

## HomeMade (Mable Sister Company)

**Website:** [homemadesupport.com.au](https://homemadesupport.com.au/)

**Model:** Self-Management Platform

### What They Do

HomeMade helps people self-manage their Support at Home funding. It's designed for those who want control over their care but with platform support.

### Product Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Provider Network** | Access to 3,000+ verified service providers |
| **Budget Management** | Transparency and control over funding |
| **Care Plan Setup** | Help establishing and managing care plans |

### Service Tiers

1. **Self-Management** - You choose providers, set schedule, control budget
2. **Fully Managed** - Expert team manages everything, you approve providers

### Target Market

Home Care Package holders who want self-management with guardrails.

---

## Five Good Friends (FGF)

**Website:** [fivegoodfriends.com.au](https://www.fivegoodfriends.com.au/)

**Founded:** 2016 | **Model:** Tech-Enabled Provider

### What They Do

Australia's first technology-enabled, data-driven home care provider. They combine skilled care teams with proprietary technology to deliver high-quality care.

### Product Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Member App** | Book services, view visits, manage care from mobile |
| **Lookout Remote Care Monitoring** | Proprietary platform for care observations and notes |
| **Care Team Profiles** | View and contact Helpers and Community Managers |
| **Collaborative Notebook** | Transparent communication between Members, families, Helpers |
| **Automated Invoicing** | Streamlined invoices on visit completion |
| **Help Plans** | Structured care planning visible to all stakeholders |

### Membership Options

1. **Self-Managed** - Control with app support
2. **Full Service** - Fully-managed care coordination

### Scale

- 4,000+ vetted care and health professionals
- First Australian home care provider with Remote Care Monitoring

### Strengths

- Technology-first approach
- Strong accessibility focus
- Transparent communication tools
- Compliant with Aged Care Quality Standards & NDIS Quality and Safeguards

### Key Differentiator

Built their own technology (Lookout) alongside operations - product and service evolved together.

## The Lookout Way

**Website:** [thelookoutway.com](https://thelookoutway.com/)

**Model:** B2B SaaS Platform

### What They Do

Home care management platform built by care providers, for care providers. Developed alongside Five Good Friends' operations.

### Product Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Rostering & Scheduling** | Full-time, part-time, casual, contractor models |
| **Quick-Fill** | AI-ranked suggestions for vacant visits |
| **Matching Algorithm** | Geography, qualifications, certifications, soft preferences, care history |
| **Award Compliance** | Automated shift calculations with breaks |
| **Claims & Statements** | Financial management and billing |
| **Remote Care Monitoring** | Observations, notes, family visibility |
| **Compliance & Reporting** | Built-in governance tools |
| **Client Plan Management** | Care planning and tracking |
| **Mobile Workforce App** | Worker-facing mobile experience |

### Integrations

- 600+ app integrations
- Services Australia API certified (secure government data sync)
- Client contribution, budget, and funding source sync

### Vision Rostering

Advanced AI-powered rostering with:
- Availability optimization
- Award compliance automation
- Profitability analysis

### Target Market

Aged care and NDIS home care providers seeking end-to-end operational software.

### Strengths

- Built from real operational experience
- Government API certification (Support at Home ready)
- Modern cloud architecture
- Connects all stakeholders (workers, clients, families, care team)

## Competitive Positioning

| Competitor | Model | Target User | Key Strength |
|------------|-------|-------------|--------------|
