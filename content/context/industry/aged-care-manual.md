## What It Covers

The manual provides comprehensive guidance on:

- Program overview and objectives
- Eligibility and assessment processes
- Service delivery requirements
- Funding and payment arrangements
- Quality and compliance standards
- Reporting obligations

---

## Why It Matters

This manual is the authoritative source for understanding:

- How the Support at Home program works
- What providers must do to comply
- How funding flows through the system
- Service categories and pricing

**All product and engineering decisions should align with the requirements in this manual.**
