## The Trilogy Care OS

The Trilogy Care OS is the ecosystem of applications, services, and integrations that enable Trilogy Care to deliver aged care services at scale. **cs** — this documentation site, built on Nuxt 4 + Nuxt Content 3

- **TC Accounts** — centralised identity and authentication
- **External integrations** — Services Australia, MYOB, Twilio, Intercom, and more

### Principles

We follow [DHH and Taylor Otwell's philosophies](/developer-docs/explanation/laravel-principles) for building software:

1. **The Majestic Monolith** — integrated, cohesive applications over microservices
2. **Convention Over Configuration** — follow established patterns, reduce decision fatigue
3. **Progress Over Perfection** — ship MVP, iterate based on feedback
4. **Boring Technology** — proven, stable tools over shiny new things
5. **Code is Read More Than Written** — optimise for readability and maintainability
