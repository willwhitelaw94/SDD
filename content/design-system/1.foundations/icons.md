---
title: "Icons"
description: "Monicon-powered icon system with Heroicons, Material, and custom SVGs"
---

Icons are managed by [Monicon](https://monicon.dev/) (`@monicon/vue`) and registered in `icons.ts` (322 icons). They render via the `<CommonIcon>` component as inline SVGs, tree-shaken at build time. Browse all available icons at [icones.js.org](https://icones.js.org/).

The icon set is defined in the [Figma Iconography page](https://www.figma.com/design/ojTCI9yefnl9ORYEVrv2WJ/Trilogy-Care-Design-System?node-id=40-8812). Click any icon below to copy its `<CommonIcon>` usage.
