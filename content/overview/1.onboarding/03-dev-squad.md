## Your First Day

Getting started is a process. Our goal is to give you meaningful traction early.

**What a successful first day looks like:**

- Group onboarding with other new starters
- Office tour (or virtual intro if remote)
- Meet the team and your squad
- Dev environment setup begins
- Lunch
- Battle plan for the week ahead

---

## Your First Week

There's compliance training to complete in aged care. Most of your first week will be training and environment setup.

| Task                  | Description                              |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Employment Hero       | Docs and policies                        |
| LMS Courses           | Compliance training                      |
| Alis Course           | Aged care specific                       |
| Dev environment setup | Clone repos, configure local environment |
| Meet stakeholders     | Designers, POs, BAs who you'll work with |
| Codebase tour         | Walk through the TC Portal architecture  |
| Ask questions         | Loads of them                            |

We'll break up the training by shadowing standups and getting familiar with Linear.

---

## Your Second Week

Now that you know the team and have your environment ready, time to start coding.

| Task              | Description                                    |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| First bug fix     | Small ticket to get familiar with the workflow |
| PR process        | Learn our review and merge workflow            |
| Feature deep-dive | Understand a feature area in detail            |
| Pair programming  | Work alongside your dev buddy                  |
| Ask questions     | Keep asking                                    |

---

## Week 3 Onwards

From here you'll be picking up feature work and contributing to sprints. Fresh eyes are valuable—call out anything that could be improved, refactored, or fixed.
