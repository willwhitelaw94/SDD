## Your First Day

Getting started is a process. Our goal is to give you meaningful traction early, without overloading you.

**What a successful first day looks like:**

- Group onboarding with other new starters
- Office tour
- Introduced to the team and your squad
- Lunch
- Battle plan for the week ahead

---

## Your First Week

There's a pile of onboarding training to do in aged care to be compliant. So strap yourself in. Most of this first week will be doing this training and meeting people.

| Task                | Description                                                 |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Employment Hero     | Docs and policies                                           |
| LMS Courses         | Compliance training                                         |
| Alis Course         | Aged care specific                                          |
| Meet stakeholders   | Everyone who touches the portal                             |
| 1:1s with designers | 30 min "what I do / what I'm working on" with each designer |
| Ask questions       | Loads of them                                               |

We'll break up the boring stuff by shadowing meetings with a design buddy, getting a tour of Jira, and lunching together.

---

## Your Second Week

Now that you know some people and some things, it's time to start what you actually came here for—Design.

| Task                | Description                               |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Provision software  | Figma, Loom, Dovetail, Claude             |
| Figma Safari        | Tour of our design system with a designer |
| First design ticket | Kick off your first real work             |
| Ask questions       | Keep asking                               |

---

## Week 3 Onwards

From here on out we'll be crushing the backlog side by side. You have fresh eyes—ask questions, call out anything that could be better, bigger, or fixed.
