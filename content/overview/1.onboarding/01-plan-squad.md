## Your First Day

Getting started is a process. Our goal is to give you context and connections early.

**What a successful first day looks like:**

- Group onboarding with other new starters
- Office tour
- Meet the team and your domain stakeholders
- Introduction to TC Portal (demo)
- Lunch
- Battle plan for the week ahead

## Planning Workflow

### From Idea to Delivery

1. **Idea** - Business need identified
2. **Discovery** - Stakeholder interviews, user research
3. **Specification** - User stories, acceptance criteria
4. **Design** - Wireframes, prototypes (with Design team)
5. **Refinement** - Technical feasibility, estimation (with Dev team)
6. **Development** - Build and test
7. **Release** - Deploy and monitor

### Writing Good User Stories

```text
As a [type of user]
I want [goal/desire]
So that [benefit/value]

Acceptance Criteria:
- Given [context], when [action], then [outcome]
- ...
```
