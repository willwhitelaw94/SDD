## Founding Story

James Whitelaw established Trilogy Care in 2020 after a personal experience. While helping his parents-in-law Peter and Inese navigate aged care in Far North Queensland, he discovered they could only access 5.5 weekly care hours through traditional providers despite their $24,000 annual package. By exploring self-managed options, the family doubled their care hours and hired local, trusted caregivers—proving the model worked better.

## Growth & Scale

From one family's solution, Trilogy Care has grown into one of Australia's leading self-managed home care providers. See [Product Mission](/overview/foundations/01-product-mission#trilogy-care-at-a-glance) for current scale metrics.

## Brand Ambassador

Paula Duncan, an acclaimed Australian actor with eight Logie Awards, serves as brand ambassador. After a serious home accident requiring rehabilitation and mobility support, she experienced how Trilogy Care's coordinated services enabled her recovery while maintaining independence.

## Core Values

- **Choice & Control**: Clients direct their own care decisions
- **Transparency**: Reduced provider fees mean more care hours
- **Community Connection**: Emphasis on staying home while staying connected
- **Personalization**: Culturally aligned and relationship-focused support
