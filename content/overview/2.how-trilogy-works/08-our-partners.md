## Our Coordinators

Trilogy Care works with **250+ independent coordinators** across Australia — small businesses that operate in a franchisee-style model under the Trilogy Care umbrella.

These coordinators:
- Run their own care coordination businesses
- Use Trilogy Care's systems, compliance framework, and support infrastructure
- Deliver Fully Coordinated (SM+) services to clients in their local areas
- Maintain direct relationships with clients while backed by Trilogy's scale

This model enables personalised, local service delivery while maintaining consistent quality and compliance standards nationally.

## Priority One Partners

Our **Priority One** partnership program recognises preferred suppliers who work with Trilogy Care in a mutually beneficial relationship.

These include:
- **Platform providers** — Technology and service platforms that integrate with our ecosystem
- **Nursing agencies** — Allied health and clinical service providers
- **Specialist suppliers** — Equipment, transport, and support service providers

Priority One partners benefit from referral relationships and preferred status, while Trilogy Care clients gain access to vetted, quality suppliers at competitive rates.
