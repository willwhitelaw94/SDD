## Our Value Proposition

Trilogy Care empowers older Australians to **self-manage their Home Care Packages**, giving them more choice, more care hours, and more control.

## What Makes Us Different

### More Care Hours

Traditional providers take large management fees, leaving less for actual care. With Trilogy Care's self-management model, clients typically receive **twice the care hours** for the same funding.

### Choice & Control

Clients choose:

- **Their own carers** — people they know and trust
- **Their own services** — what they actually need
- **Their own schedule** — when care happens

### Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees. Clients see exactly where their funding goes:

- Clear breakdown of all costs
- Real-time budget tracking
- Monthly statements

**Typical hourly rates for popular in-home services:**

| Model | Rate | Savings |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Self Managed** | $66/hour | ~50% less than traditional |
| **Fully Coordinated** | $79/hour | ~41% less than traditional |
| Other Providers (avg) | $135/hour | — |

*Rates are averages from the last 60 days. Self-managed clients can negotiate even lower rates directly with their chosen workers.*

### Local, Trusted Carers

Rather than being assigned random workers, clients can engage:

- Family and friends as paid carers
- Local support workers they already know
- Specialists of their choosing

## The Trilogy Care Difference
