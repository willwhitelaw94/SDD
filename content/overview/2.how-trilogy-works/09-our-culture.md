Culture is how we work together, not just what we work on. At Trilogy Care, we've built an environment where people can do their best work while genuinely caring about each other and the people we serve.

---

## Open Door Policy

There's no "right channel" to raise an idea or concern. Talk to anyone.

- **Leaders are accessible** - From team leads to executives, doors are open
- **No gatekeeping** - If you have a question, ask. If you see a problem, say it
- **Skip levels welcome** - Direct communication is encouraged, not frowned upon
- **Feedback flows both ways** - We want to hear what's working and what isn't

::callout{icon="i-lucide-message-circle"}
**The best ideas come from anywhere.** Don't wait for permission to contribute.
::

---

## Flat Structure

Titles exist for clarity, not hierarchy. We're small enough that everyone has a voice.

| What We Avoid       | What We Embrace       |
| ------------------- | --------------------- |
| Bureaucracy         | Direct action         |
| Approval chains     | Trust and autonomy    |
| "That's not my job" | Collective ownership  |
| Politics            | Transparent decisions |

Everyone is expected to:

- **Contribute beyond their role** when needed
- **Make decisions** within their domain
- **Challenge ideas** respectfully, regardless of who proposed them
- **Help teammates** even when it's not their responsibility

## Passion for What We Do

We're not just building software—we're helping older Australians live better lives.

### Why This Work Matters

- Every feature we ship affects real people
- Our clients are someone's parent, grandparent, neighbour
- Self-managed care gives people **choice** and **dignity**
- We're transforming an industry that's historically underserved by technology

### How Passion Shows Up

- **In conversations** - People care about the outcome, not just the task
- **In quality** - We don't ship work we're not proud of
- **In problem-solving** - We dig deeper because we want to get it right
- **In each other** - We invest in teammates' growth and wellbeing

## What You Can Expect

### From Leadership

- Transparency about company direction and challenges
- Investment in your growth
- Trust to do your job without micromanagement
- Recognition when you do great work

### From Your Team

- Support when you're stuck
- Honest feedback to help you improve
- Collaboration over competition
- Celebration of wins, big and small

### From Yourself

- Bring your whole self to work
- Speak up when something's wrong
- Help others succeed
- Take ownership of your growth

---

## Culture Add, Not Culture Fit

We don't hire people who are just like us. We hire people who **add** to our culture—bringing new perspectives, experiences, and ideas.

What matters:

- Alignment with our values
- Genuine care for the work and the people
- Willingness to learn and adapt
- Ability to give and receive feedback

What doesn't matter:

- Where you went to school
- Your exact background
- Whether you "fit the mould"
