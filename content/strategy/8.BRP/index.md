## What is BRP?

**Big Room Planning** is a quarterly ceremony that brings together all key stakeholders to:

- Set quarterly objectives and priorities
- Review team capacity and constraints
- Identify cross-team dependencies
- Align on roadmap commitments
- Surface risks early
