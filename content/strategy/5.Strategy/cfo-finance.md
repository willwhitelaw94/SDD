## Strategic Context

Trilogy Care's finance engine is strained by:
- **High-cost working capital funding** that needs optimisation
- Manual, people-dependent processes that slow claims, bill payment and customer statements
- Frequent approval delays from Services Australia compound cash flow risk
- Backlog of **"Bills on Hold"** highlighting process fragmentation across teams

---

## FY26 Objectives

| KPI | FY25 Baseline | FY26 Target | Why it matters |
|-----|---------------|-------------|----------------|
| Claims processing cycle time | 18–24 days | **< 5 days (daily claims)** | Releases cash sooner, cuts receivable ageing |
| Bill processing cost / invoice | Baseline | **-30%** | Leverage scanning + AI exception handling |
| Email query backlog | High volume | **-70%** | Introduce ticketing & triage automation |
| Finance FTE redeployment | – | **20% shifted to value-add analytics** | Free capacity for commercial partnering |

---

## Strategic Pillars

### Working Capital & Claims Excellence
*Cut finance cost of capital; accelerate cash*

- **Transition to daily Services Australia claims** and automate post-claim reconciliations
- **Optimise funding facilities** – reduce limits and explore lower-cost alternatives

### End-to-end Process Automation
*Remove manual bottlenecks; lift accuracy*

- **Portal bill processing with AI scanning & exception routing**
- **AP/AR automation** including e-invoicing and direct debit ITF collections
- **Supplier verification for fraud protection**

### Data-driven Insight & Partnering
*Turn finance into a proactive advisor*

- Build a **Business Hub** (live dashboards & Stewart Brown benchmarking)
- Embed **Commercial Business Partners** to work with each department on KPIs, budgeting & scenario modelling

### Governance & Risk Management
*Strengthen controls for a "large-entity" audit environment*

- Roll out **delegation of authority (DoA) matrix** & purchase order limits
- Automate expense approvals
- Re-engineer MYOB for speed & integrity

### People & Capability Uplift
*Shift effort from processing to insight*

- Recruit an **experienced Payroll Manager** and redeploy current officer to HR
- Stand up a **Financial Reporting (FP&A) squad**
- Grow consumer data accountants in line with volume

## Organisational Redesign

| Team | Change |
|------|--------|
| **Finance – Payroll** | Dedicated manager to own compliance, value-add projects and HR alignment |
| **Finance – Commercial Business Partners** | Analysts assigned to operating divisions; co-develop Business Hub reporting with Data team |
| **Finance – FP&A** | Upgrade from classical accounting to scenario-based planning, automation and dashboard self-service |
| **Consumer Finance & Banking** | Scale assistant accountant pool as transaction volume rises under Support at Home |

## Key Technology Enablers

1. **Portal Scanning + AI exceptions** – target ≥ 80% straight-through invoice processing
2. **Centralised Data Warehouse & Contracts Register** – single source for spend, claims, approvals
3. **Ticketing system (emails, phone, internal requests)** to cut query turnaround below 24h
4. **Business Hub dashboards** feeding real-time metrics to leadership and commercial partners
