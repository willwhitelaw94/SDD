## Strategic Context

Trilogy Care's workforce is young, diverse, and growing — but faces retention challenges:

- **Early-career turnover** — The majority of exits happen during probation and in younger cohorts
- **Root causes** — Mismatch between role expectations, limited on-the-job coaching, uneven leadership support

---

| KPI | FY25 Baseline | FY26 Target | Why it matters |
|-----|---------------|-------------|----------------|

| Annualised turnover | Current rate | **≤ 35%** | Stabilise workforce & protect knowledge |

## Strategic Pillars

### Recruitment & Onboarding Quality
*Hire the right people, first time*

- **Robust recruitment toolkit** — refreshed position descriptions, job grading, realistic job previews, assessments
- **Assessment centre format** for high-volume roles
- **"Stay-or-Struggle" checkpoints** early in tenure to flag risk

### Early-career Engagement
*Turn churn into commitment*

- Structured **career development and mentoring** tracks
- Social connection events linked to mission and values
- Clear progression pathways

### Leadership Capability & Culture
*Managers who coach, not churn*

- Launch **Front-Line Leader program** covering feedback, coaching, task prioritisation
- Monthly manager huddles to review retention and action plans
- Leadership accountability for team outcomes

### People Systems & Analytics
*Data-driven P&C*

- Expand HRIS usage to cover requisitions, approvals, reviews, remuneration
- Stand up **turnover and tenure analytics** with drill-down by manager, cohort, and reason
- Real-time dashboards for leadership

### Policy, Structure & Reward
*Foundation for scale*

- Complete **policy refresh** and publish single knowledge base
- Implement **grading and banding framework** with transparent ranges
- Align P&C structure to support business growth
