## Strategic Context

Trilogy Care's Operations & Quality function shoulders two simultaneous pressures:

1. **Escalating regulatory complexity** – The team intends to mirror the Commonwealth Department of Health's risk-based regulatory playbook across all suppliers to stay ahead of new Aged Care Act obligations

2. **High operational variability** – Care partner workloads swing wildly with backlogs in emails, bills on hold, and overdue check-ins. This is caused by inconsistent guidance and tooling, driving uneven client experience.

## Strategic Pillars

### Risk-based Compliance Management
*Anticipate, not react*

Embed the **risk-stratified regulatory framework** used by DoH across suppliers and internal teams.

### Proactive Quality Assurance
*Detect issues early*

Launch an **integrated ticketing system** with:
- Scheduled sample audits
- AI anomaly/risk detection
- Dashboards covering business health, bill-payment accuracy and complaint analytics

### Process & Policy Modernisation
*"Single source of truth" tooling*

End-to-end rewrite of **emails, policies and processes** for Support at Home:
- Rebuild claims, budgets, collections and statement flows

### Operational Efficiency & Growth Readiness
*Scale safely at 30% YoY*

Deploy intelligent tools to drive an **18% efficiency lift**, allowing:
- Improved CP:CR ratios
- Supporting horizontal growth

### Workforce & Capability Uplift
*Right people, right seats*

Increase Operations & Quality headcount dedicated to regulatory response.

## Operating Model Adjustments

| Change | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Contact Excellence Team** | Created inside Quality to oversee ticket triage, root-cause analyses and cross-functional feedback loops |
| **Quality & Ops Pod** | Formalised, reporting to CQOO; each pod owns a recipient cohort, supplier cohort and queue of compliance artefacts |
| **Regulatory response reserve** | Buffer capacity protects BAU during ACQSC or GPMS escalations |

---

## Technology Enablers

1. **AI-enabled ticketing suite** (risk scoring, anomaly detection)
2. **Portal bill-payment validation** to cut hold rates
3. **Business health & risk dashboards** surfaced to exec and frontline in near-real time
4. **Policy & Process RAG engine** to flag outdated controls continuously
