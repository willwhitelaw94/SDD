## FY26 Strategic Theme

**Scale efficiently while transforming for Support at Home.**

Every department is focused on:
- **Automation** — Reduce manual effort, increase throughput
- **Data-driven decisions** — Single source of truth, real-time insights
- **Compliance readiness** — Meet new Aged Care Act obligations
- **Growth enablement** — Support 30%+ YoY customer growth

## How Strategy Connects to Your Work

Every feature, fix, and initiative should ladder up to one of these strategic pillars:

1. **Does it reduce manual effort?** → Automation
2. **Does it improve data quality or visibility?** → Data-driven decisions
3. **Does it help us meet regulatory requirements?** → Compliance readiness
4. **Does it support more customers or better service?** → Growth enablement

When scoping work, reference the relevant department strategy to understand the "why" behind priorities.
