## Strategic Context

Trilogy Care's growth engine is positioned around clear **4P differentiators**:
- **Self-managed package choice**
- **Low fees that convey value**
- **Performance-driven multi-channel marketing**
- **Nationwide digital + personal support**

The company has sustained a **237% CAGR**, making it Australia's fastest-growing HCP provider.

| KPI | FY26 Target | Rationale |

## Strategic Pillars

### Performance Marketing Excellence
*Drive efficient volume while preserving CAC/CPL*

- **"Nail the funnel"**: build end-to-end CVR, CPL, CAC, ROMI models
- Deploy market research for sharper audience insights
- Introduce competitor tracking capabilities

### Brand Equity & Awareness
*Cement values of Value, Choice, Ease, Trust and widen top-of-funnel reach*

- Allocate **1% of revenue to brand marketing**
- Implement brand tracking metrics
- Engage PR ambassadors & run DRTV/BVOD/SVOD pilots
- Refresh video assets & complete website audit/upgrade

### Sales & BD Expansion
*Convert growing lead flow and deepen partner channels*

- **Split CSR team into pods of 6 FTE**, add TL & senior reps; appoint Head of Sales
- **Upscale BD**: create BD TL, Relationship Manager, Head of Partnerships
- Optimise partner handover & incentive structure

### Insight-led Decisioning
*Precision in spend, segmentation & retention*

- Stand up structured A/B testing for lead capture
- Activate KPI display & gamification
- Deploy **AI knowledge repository (RAG)** and real-time call coaching/prompts
- Implement lead scoring & recipient retention-risk scoring models

### Ecosystem & Channel Innovation
*Broaden routes to market & engagement formats*

- Run roadshow series and mall pop-up trials
- Produce reform-ready customer assets & comms
- Roll out AI-generated blogs/newsletters
