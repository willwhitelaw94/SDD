## Strategic Context

- **Self-managed home care is exploding.** Reforms and consumer preference are expected to lift the segment to **20% of the entire HCP market (~A$1.4bn)**
- Trilogy is targeting **~4.6% market share** in self-managed by Jun-26
- High **barriers to entry** (working capital, compliance, sophisticated payments tech) have kept rival numbers low
- Trilogy is **the only scaled, fully-compliant pure-play provider**
- The **Support at Home (SaH) program** rewrites funding, caps care-management fees at 10%, halves rollover flexibility, and ties revenue tightly to utilisation

## Strategic Pillars

### SaH-Ready Pricing & Billing
*Make the 10% fee cap work*

Re-factor fee engine to split:
- **Care Management (10%)**
- **Service Loading (≤ 10%)**
- **Product Loading (≤ 5%)**

Preserving per-customer revenue at ≥ old 15% levels when utilisation ≥ 75%.

### Utilisation-Driven Engagement
*Turn revenue risk into product stickiness*

- In-portal **utilisation dashboard & nudging logic** for coordinators and recipients
- Automated "use-it-or-lose-it" alerts tied to quarterly budgets

### Marketplace Expansion
*Broaden SKU & supplier footprint*

- Build **Service Provider Directory** with searchable verified profiles (filters, ratings, fees)
- Integrate assistive tech and home mods vendors
- Enable product loading at checkout

### Competitive Intelligence & Differentiation
*Stay ahead of incumbents & insurgents*

- Continuous competitor feature benchmarking (digital & pricing)
- Strategic positioning (Ansoff matrix) to guide roadmap bets

### Compliance & Risk Safeguards
*Meet new Aged Care Act obligations*

- Embed worker verification APIs
- Price cap enforcement
- Automated audit trails to remain "fully compliant" at scale
