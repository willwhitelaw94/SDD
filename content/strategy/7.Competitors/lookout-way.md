## Platform Overview

The Lookout Way is a SaaS care management platform used by 100+ Australian care providers. Originally built internally by Five Good Friends, it was spun out in 2021 as a standalone product.

### Core Features

- **Rostering & Scheduling** - Staff allocation and shift management
- **Family Portal** - Real-time visibility for families into care delivery
- **Automated Escalation** - Workflow triggers for incidents and concerns
- **Compliance Tracking** - Regulatory adherence and reporting
- **Mobile App** - Care worker companion app

### Target Market

- Aged care providers (primary focus)
- Home Care Package providers
- NDIS providers (secondary)

---

## Why They Matter

### Dual Business Model

Five Good Friends operates both as:
1. **Care Provider** - Delivering care directly (top 20 nationally)
2. **Platform Vendor** - Selling Lookout Way to other providers

This creates a unique competitive dynamic - they sell software to providers they also compete with for clients.

### TPG Investment (April 2025)

TPG acquired a majority stake, bringing:
- Significant growth capital
- M&A capability for acquisitions
- Operational expertise scaling SaaS

### Market Position

- 100+ providers on platform
- Growing faster than traditional software vendors
- Technology-first positioning

## Competitive Analysis

### What They Do Well

- **UX/Design** - Modern, clean interface
- **Family Engagement** - Strong family portal features
- **Integration** - Built for Australian care context
- **Support at Home Ready** - Already preparing for new program

### Potential Weaknesses

- **Conflict of Interest** - Providers wary of competitor's software
- **Aged Care Focus** - Less NDIS capability
- **Scale** - Still building market share
