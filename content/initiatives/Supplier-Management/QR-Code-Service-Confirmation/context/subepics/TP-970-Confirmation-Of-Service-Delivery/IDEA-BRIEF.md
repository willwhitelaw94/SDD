**Epic Code**: Confirmation of Service Delivery
**Created**: 2026-01-14

## Problem Statement (What)

- Compliance requirements mandate documentation of care and service delivery confirmation
- Current system lacks clear mechanism for workers to confirm service delivery at participant locations
- Support at Home reforms require QR code or sign-in evidence of service completion
- Manual sign-in books are inefficient and lack digital integration
- No automated way to capture and validate service delivery evidence

## Possible Solution (How)

Implement QR code sign-in mechanism for service confirmation at participant locations
- Create mobile-friendly check-in interface for care workers
- Integrate sign-in data with service delivery records
- Establish automated compliance verification based on sign-in evidence
- Provide reporting and audit trails for regulatory compliance

**Before**: Manual sign-in books; disconnected verification; compliance gaps

**After**: Digital QR code sign-in; automated verification; clear compliance records

## Benefits (Why)

- Meets Support at Home compliance requirements for service delivery confirmation
- Eliminates manual sign-in book management
- Provides digital proof of service delivery
- Enables automated compliance tracking and reporting
- Improves operational efficiency for care coordinators
