**Epic Code**: Shopping List
**Created**: 2026-01-14

## Problem Statement (What)

- Users cannot easily compile and share shopping lists for home modifications and supplies
- Current system lacks a curated approach to managing multiple procurement items
- Coordination between carers, suppliers, and participants is inefficient for product selection
- No centralized way to track and compare pricing across potential suppliers

## Possible Solution (How)

Create shopping list feature allowing users to compile items needed
- Integrate with supplier catalog and pricing information
- Enable shopping list sharing across care team members
- Provide pricing comparison and supplier recommendations

**Before**: Manual item tracking; fragmented supplier information; coordination challenges

**After**: Centralized shopping lists; integrated pricing; easy sharing and coordination

## Benefits (Why)

- Simplifies procurement process for home modifications and supplies
- Enables efficient supplier comparison and cost management
- Improves coordination among care team members
- Provides visibility into planned expenditures
- Reduces manual coordination overhead
