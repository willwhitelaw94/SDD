## Objectives

### Purpose of Communication

- To ensure all internal teams and external suppliers are informed of upcoming Home Modifications Portal (HMS) changes, including new UI features and updated processes.
- To provide clear, consistent, and accessible information that enables stakeholders to understand what is changing, why it is changing, and how it impacts their workflow.
- To support a smooth transition from existing systems to the Supplier Portal by preparing stakeholders ahead of testing and deployment.

### Outcomes to Achieve

- Stakeholders understand the new or updated HMS processes and user interface and can confidently perform their required tasks in the Supplier Portal.
- Reduction in avoidable queries, errors, and support requests during and after rollout due to clear and proactive communication.
- Successful coordination of testing (UAT) and deployment activities through timely notification to relevant teams.
- Consistent messaging across all departments to support alignment, minimise confusion, and ensure correct use of the new system.
- Improved supplier and staff experience through streamlined communication and clarity on responsibilities and process changes.

## Feedback & Monitoring

**Internal Teams (Compliance, Care, Operations, BD)**

- **UAT feedback channels** (Teams chat, feedback form, or email summary) during testing to gather issues, questions, and improvement suggestions.
- **Team leader debrief** sessions after UAT and post-deployment to gather consolidated feedback from each department.
- **Support inbox monitoring** (e.g., helpdesk or shared mailbox) to track recurring questions or confusion points.

**Suppliers**

- **Supplier Helpdesk / shared mailbox monitoring** to identify trends in questions or issues raised after deployment.
- **Bounce rates and open rates** from emails to measure whether suppliers are receiving and engaging with the communication.

### How effectiveness will be measured

- **Reduction in support tickets** related to portal use, compared with previous system updates.
- **Volume and type of issues raised during UAT**—fewer process confusion issues indicates communication clarity.
- **Post-deployment error rates** (e.g., incorrect submissions, misused UI features) tracked by Compliance or Operations.
- **Survey scores** from internal staff and suppliers indicating understanding and confidence using the new processes.
- **Email engagement metrics** (open rate, click-through rate).
- **Successful completion of tasks** by suppliers/staff in the updated portal without needing follow-up clarification.
- **Feedback from team leads** confirming their teams feel prepared and informed.
