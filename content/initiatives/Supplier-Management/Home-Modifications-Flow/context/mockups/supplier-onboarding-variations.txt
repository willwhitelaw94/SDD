## Recommendation

**For Product Supplier**: **OPTION B: Wizard with Progress**
- Simple, guided experience appropriate for straightforward registration
- Progress indicator builds confidence
- Category descriptions help first-time users
- Review step prevents submission errors

**For Contractor**: **OPTION E: Accordion/Expandable Sections**
- Handles complexity better than wizard (many states = many steps)
- See entire scope of registration at once
- Jump to incomplete sections easily
- Progress indicator motivates completion
- Works well when contractors may not complete in one session (can see what's left)

**Alternative for Contractor**: **OPTION D: Multi-Step Wizard** if contractors prefer more guidance and don't mind more steps.
