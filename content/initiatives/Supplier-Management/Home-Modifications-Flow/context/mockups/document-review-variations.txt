## Recommendation

**Primary Interface: OPTION B - Side-by-Side Document List with Quick Actions**

Recommended because:
1. **Efficient workflow**: See all documents + current document in one view
2. **Quick actions**: Approve/reject without navigation
3. **Dropdown reasons**: Common rejection reasons speed decisions
4. **Context visible**: Document list shows overall progress
5. **Scalable**: Works for 3 documents or 10 documents

**For Multi-Quote Scenarios: OPTION E - Comparison View**
- Critical for validating independent quotes requirement
- Side-by-side comparison prevents errors
- Price difference highlighted for CP decision-making

**For Power Users: OPTION C - Full-Screen Review Mode**
- Keyboard shortcuts enable very fast batch processing
- Good for compliance team members who review 50+ documents/day

**For First-Time/Occasional Users: OPTION D - Batch Review Checklist Mode**
- Guided checklist reduces training time
- Clear progress indicator
- Inline previews + checklists build confidence
