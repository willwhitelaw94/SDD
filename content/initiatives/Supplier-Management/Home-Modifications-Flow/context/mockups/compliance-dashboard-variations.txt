## Recommendation

**OPTION F: Accordion List with Embedded Actions** is recommended because:

1. **Best Balance**: Combines overview and detail without overwhelming
2. **Efficient Review**: Expand only what you need, inline actions reduce clicks
3. **Grouped by Supplier**: Makes sense for compliance team's mental model
4. **Progress Visibility**: Progress bars and document counts visible at collapsed level
5. **Scalable**: Works well with 5 works or 50 works
6. **Action-Oriented**: All approval actions are inline, no navigation required

**Alternative Recommendation**: **OPTION B: Compact Table with Expandable Rows** for users who prefer high information density and need to process many works quickly.

**For Mobile/Tablet**: **OPTION A: Card Grid** would work better with responsive stacking.
