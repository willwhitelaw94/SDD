## Problem Statement (What)

- Payment processing for suppliers is time-consuming and subject to delays - Current payment workflows lack flexibility for urgent or expedited payments - Suppliers may experience cash flow challenges due to payment delays - No mechanism for priority/express payment handling when needed

## Possible Solution (How)

### Approach - Implement express payment processing option for eligible suppliers - Create priority payment workflows with faster settlement times - Establish criteria for express payment eligibility - Integrate with billing and financial systems for streamlined processing ### Before/After - **Before**: Standard payment timelines; inflexible processing; supplier cash flow challenges - **After**: Express payment options; faster settlement; improved supplier relationships

## Benefits (Why)

- Improves supplier satisfaction and retention - Enables flexible payment solutions for business partners - Supports supplier cash flow management - Creates competitive advantage in supplier recruitment - Reduces payment processing friction
