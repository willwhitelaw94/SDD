## Problem Statement (What)

Invoices V2 is a upgrade to Trilogy Care's invoice processing system, delivering a fully integrated, automation-driven pipeline from invoice receipt through to payment and claims submission. This initiative consolidates four key BRP action items — **Invoices V2**, **Query Invoice**, **Auto-Approve Invoice**, and **Dispute Invoice** — into a single, cohesive workflow that blends AI-based anomaly detection, structured resolution processes, and automation to reduce manual workload, improve payment timeliness, and ensure compliance with the Support at Home (SAH) framework.

## Benefits (Why)

**Effort & Cost** Medium–high complexity: multi-sprint build with cross-team dependencies (product, dev, data, ops). Additional design and engineering resources for automation rules, UI, and MYOB/claims integration. **Timeframe** Discovery: 2–3 weeks. Development & Testing: ~2–3 sprints (6–9 weeks). Target Launch: Post-Budget V2 go-live, aligned with SAH operational readiness. **Benefits Realisation** Reduce average invoice processing time by 30–50%. Cut invoices pending approval >5 days to below 10% of total. Improve client billing satisfaction scores in post-launch survey.

## Assumptions & Dependencies, Risks

### Assumptions
**Assumptions** SAH funding rules and data structures are stable post-Budget V2. AI/RAG automation achieves sufficient accuracy to reliably flag issues. Users are trained to distinguish between "query" (internal resolution) and "dispute" (formal challenge). **Dependencies** Budget V2 completion and migration of all client packages. Integration with MYOB for financial sync. Alignment with claims submission workflows and deadlines. **Risks** Auto-approving invoices without adequate anomaly checks could lead to incorrect payments. Excessive use of "dispute" by clients could slow payment cycles. Dependence on API readiness; may require extended CSV fallback.
