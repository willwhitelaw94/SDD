# Portal Bill Submission

Self-service invoice submission portal enabling suppliers to submit invoices directly, eliminating email/manual submission workflows.

## Problem

Suppliers submit invoices via email requiring manual data entry by the Philippines team (50 people processing ~100 bills/day), creating delays and errors.

## Solution

Self-service submission portal in the Supplier Portal:

1. **Invoice Upload** — Upload PDF, AI extracts line items
2. **Pre-Validation** — Check client, services, rates before submit
3. **Status Tracking** — Real-time visibility: Submitted → Review → Approved → Paid
4. **Bill Edit Integration** — Pre-created bills flow directly into processing queue

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Supplier adoption | 50% of contracted suppliers |
| Invoice-to-payment time | -50% |
| Manual data entry reduction | -70% |
