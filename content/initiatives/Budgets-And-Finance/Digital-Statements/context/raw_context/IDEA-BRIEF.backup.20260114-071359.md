## Problem Statement (What)

- Participants and care coordinators rely on paper-based financial statements - Manual statement generation is time-consuming and error-prone - Statements lack real-time budget visibility and detailed transaction information - No digital delivery mechanism for statements to participants - Regulatory reporting requires manual compilation of statement data

## Possible Solution (How)

### Approach - Implement digital statement generation system integrated with budget management - Create real-time statement views for both participants and care coordinators - Enable digital delivery of statements via portal and email - Develop comprehensive transaction history and filtering capabilities - Integrate with QR code statements for enhanced participant access ### Before/After - **Before**: Paper-based statements; manual generation; limited visibility; delayed delivery - **After**: Digital statements; real-time views; automated generation; instant delivery

## Benefits (Why)

- Provides real-time budget and transaction visibility for participants - Reduces manual statement preparation workload for staff - Enables faster statement delivery and access for participants - Improves financial transparency and participant engagement - Supports regulatory compliance with audit trails and digital records
