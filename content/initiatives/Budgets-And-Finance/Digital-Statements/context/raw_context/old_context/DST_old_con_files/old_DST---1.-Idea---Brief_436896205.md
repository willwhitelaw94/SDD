<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Idea</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Implement an interactive <strong>Digital Statement</strong> feature in the Trilogy Care portal to replace or enhance current static multi-page PDF statements. The solution will:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Display funding streams, service bookings, participant co-payments, and coordination fees in a structured, compliant format.</p></li>
<li><p>Include a <strong>digital transaction table</strong> allowing users to filter/group by funding stream, hyperlink to source documents, and lodge disputes directly from the portal.</p></li>
<li><p>Generate <strong>Support at Home–compliant monthly statements</strong> by the required deadline (last day of the following month), showing remaining budgets, contributions, and service utilisation.</p></li>
<li><p>Allow integration with coordinator fee dashboards and supplier billing workflows to ensure all financial data is centralised and transparent.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Key Functions</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Benefits</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><ol>
<li><p><strong>Compliance:</strong> Meets Support at Home regulatory requirements for monthly statements and financial transparency.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Operational Efficiency:</strong> Reduces manual statement generation and distribution workload; integrates with existing billing and budget modules.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Client Empowerment:</strong> Gives recipients and representatives real-time, self-service access to financial information.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Dispute Handling:</strong> Built-in dispute workflow shortens resolution times and reduces back-and-forth.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Coordinator Visibility:</strong> Coordinators can view fee breakdowns and income summaries directly in the portal, improving trust and accuracy.</p></li>
</ol></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Stakeholders</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><ul>
<li><p><strong>Internal:</strong> Product Team, Finance, Operations, Data Engineering, Care Coordinators, Customer Support.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>External:</strong> Recipients, Representatives, Coordinators, Suppliers, Services Australia (regulatory).</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Assumptions, Dependencies &amp; Risks</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Assumptions:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Accurate, consolidated transaction and contribution data will be available from MYOB sync, budget modules, and claims workflows.</p></li>
<li><p>Users will prefer an online, interactive statement view over (or alongside) PDFs.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Dependencies:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Budget V2 completion and migration (ensures accurate funding data).</p></li>
<li><p>Claims workflow stability (ensures correct subsidy and co-payment values).</p></li>
<li><p>MYOB/Portal sync for contributions.</p></li>
<li><p>Dispute Statement workflow readiness.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Risks:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Data integrity issues causing inaccurate statements.</p></li>
<li><p>Regulatory changes altering statement format requirements.</p></li>
<li><p>Performance concerns if statements require large transaction datasets to render in real-time.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Estimated Effort, Cost, Time and Benefits</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><ul>
<li><p><strong>Effort:</strong> Medium-High — requires backend consolidation, frontend interactive UI, dispute workflow integration, and compliance validation.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Cost:</strong> Primarily development and QA (estimate 1–2 sprints depending on backend readiness).</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Timeframe:</strong> Target Sprint 24 (late Nov) for Support at Home go-live alignment.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Expected Benefits:</strong> Compliance certainty, reduced manual work, improved satisfaction scores, stronger client and coordinator engagement.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
