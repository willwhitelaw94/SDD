This initiative covers all budget and finance-related features including budget management, invoicing, claims processing, collections, statements, and coordinator tools.

## Epics

### Budget Management
- **Budget-Reloaded** - Budget system improvements
- **Consumer-Budget-Preview** - Budget visibility for consumers
- **Coordinator-Budget-Management** - Tools for coordinators to manage budgets

### Invoicing
- **AI-Invoice-Classification** - AI-powered invoice classification
- **AI-Invoice-V3** - Invoice AI version 3
- **Invoice-Recode** - Invoice recoding functionality
- **Invoices-V2** - Invoice system version 2
- **On-Hold-Bills-Flow** - On-hold bills workflow

### Claims & Statements
- **SAH-Claims-Process** - Support at Home claims processing
- **Digital-Statements** - Digital statement generation and delivery

### Collections & Contributions
- **Collections-V1** - Initial collections functionality
- **Collections-V2** - Enhanced collections features
- **Voluntary-Contributions-Enhancements** - Improvements to voluntary contributions
