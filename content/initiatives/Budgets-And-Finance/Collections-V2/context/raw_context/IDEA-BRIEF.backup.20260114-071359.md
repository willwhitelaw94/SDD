## Problem Statement (What)

- Current collections process lacks sophisticated rules and targeting capabilities - Collection workflows are manual and time-intensive for billing teams - No intelligent prioritization or escalation mechanisms for overdue accounts - Collections data and outcomes are not systematically tracked for improvement - Compliance with fair collection practices is difficult to monitor

## Possible Solution (How)

### Approach - Implement advanced collections management system with rule-based workflows - Create intelligent account prioritization based on aging and risk assessment - Develop automated escalation and notification triggers for overdue accounts - Establish comprehensive collections tracking and reporting dashboard - Integrate compliance monitoring and fair practice enforcement ### Before/After - **Before**: Manual collections; limited tracking; inconsistent processes; compliance gaps - **After**: Automated workflows; intelligent prioritization; comprehensive tracking; compliance verified

## Benefits (Why)

- Improves collections efficiency through automated prioritization and workflows - Increases collections success rate through intelligent targeting and escalation - Reduces manual workload for billing and collections teams - Enables data-driven insights on collections performance and outcomes - Ensures compliance with fair collection practice regulations
