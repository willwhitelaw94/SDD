## Problem Statement

Clients lack a clear, compliant, and accessible view of their budgets under the new Support at Home model. Current outputs are fragmented, leading to confusion about Final Agreed Price (FAP), contributions, and procurement costs. Without a standardised client-facing budget, onboarding and migration risk delays, compliance breaches, and poor client understanding.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Solution

Develop a client-facing budget feature that:

- Generates **downloadable PDFs** for client distribution.

- Displays **FAP** as a single consolidated figure, per regulatory mandate.

- Provides **source rate transparency** (procurement cost view excluding Trilogy loadings).

- Standardises **rate card display** (weekday fees, units, frequency; supplier names excluded).

- Includes **disclaimers** for estimated funding streams and contributions, removable post–Nov 1.

- Reuses proven statement design components, with enhanced tables for procurement guidance.
