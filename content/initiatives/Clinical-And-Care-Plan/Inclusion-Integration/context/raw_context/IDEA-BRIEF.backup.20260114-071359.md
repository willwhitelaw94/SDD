## Problem Statement (What)

- Current system lacks integrated support for managing participant inclusions across care packages - Inclusion data is fragmented across multiple systems without clear visibility - Care coordinators struggle to track inclusion status and compliance - Manual tracking processes are error-prone and inconsistent - No unified view of participant inclusions for reporting and compliance purposes

## Possible Solution (How)

### Approach - Implement comprehensive inclusion management system within care planning module - Centralize inclusion data tracking with visibility across all care packages - Create automated compliance tracking for inclusion requirements - Develop reporting capabilities for inclusion status and trends - Establish audit trails for inclusion changes and updates ### Before/After - **Before**: Fragmented inclusion data; manual tracking; limited visibility; compliance gaps - **After**: Centralized inclusion management; automated tracking; complete visibility; clear compliance records

## Benefits (Why)

- Provides unified view of participant inclusions across all care packages - Reduces manual data entry and tracking burden on care coordinators - Enables automated compliance validation for inclusion requirements - Improves reporting accuracy for inclusion status and utilization - Enhances regulatory compliance through complete audit trails
