Pyramid behaviour:
  - Each tier is clickable — filters the list to that Maslow level
  - Count badge on each tier shows how many needs at that level
  - Active/selected tier is highlighted (● marker + teal accent)
  - "Clear filter" resets to show all needs (grouped by level)
  - Tiers with 0 needs are dimmed but still clickable
  - Pyramid respects the active status tab (counts reflect Active/Draft/Archived)
  - Smooth slide-down animation when revealed, slide-up when hidden
