Migrate incident management from Zoho CRM to Portal, making it the primary Incident Management System (IMS) for care partners and clinicians. Two-phase approach: Phase 1 gets incidents visible and raisable from Portal (quick win), Phase 2 adds full CRUD, SIRS workflow, auto-escalation, and incident → case automation.

## Phase Summary

| Phase | Scope | Effort | Dependencies |
|-------|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Phase 1** | Intake form, Edit.vue, client-record visibility, triage at intake, basic SIRS tracking | 2-3 sprints | Marianne's redesigned form |
| **Phase 2** | Full CRUD, auto-escalation, notifications, incident → case, risk auto-update, analytics | 3-4 sprints | CLI, RNC2 |
