## Cases Tab — Case List View — 6 Variations

Context: This is the primary working view for the Cases tab on a Package.
It sits inside PackageViewLayout, wrapped in a CommonCard.
Primary user: Clinical Lead (governance), Secondary: Coordinator (daily case work).
Design principle: "Transparency over efficiency" — show more, not less.

Badge colours:

  Status: ●Active = green  ●Escalat. = red  ●Closed = gray
  Overdue: ⚠ amber warning badge with days count

Pros: Familiar CommonTable pattern. Sortable columns. Built-in search & filter.
      Matches Incidents tab — consistency across package tabs.
Cons: Flat — all cases look equal regardless of urgency. Clinical leads must
      scan the full table to find overdue cases.

      Less structured than a table for reporting-minded clinical leads.

      across tabs. Violates "Transparency" principle.
