Medication administered at incorrect time for two consecutive days

New Received Triage Accepted Investigation Resolution Closed

Description

Mrs. Thompson's evening medication was administered at 8:45 PM on Monday and 9:15 PM on Tuesday, instead of the prescribed 7:00 PM window. The family was notified on Wednesday morning and expressed serious concern about the impact on her blood pressure management regime. The attending nurse confirmed the timing discrepancy was due to staff changeover delays.

Background

Margaret Thompson is an 82-year-old HCP Level 4 recipient with hypertension and Type 2 diabetes. Her medication schedule is time-sensitive due to interactions between her blood pressure and diabetes medications. Previous incidents in 2025 included one late administration (15 minutes) which was resolved with a schedule review. This is the first instance of consecutive-day timing errors.
