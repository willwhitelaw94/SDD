## 1. Executive Summary

<div class="table-wrap">

<table class="confluenceTable" data-table-width="906" data-layout="center" data-local-id="5dbf6e61-9acb-4b20-95ca-e78d963ce70b">
<tbody>
<tr>
<th class="confluenceTh"><p>Field</p></th>
<th class="confluenceTh"><p>Description</p></th>
</tr>
&#10;<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Problem (Why Now)</p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Currently, the recipient portal is not an optimised user-friendly website. For example, when a recipient logs in, their data is contained on one long, scrollable page and the most important package information is not immediately available at a glance. We know it is not optimised, because recent CSAT scores were 3.2 / 5 and we are aiming for 4 (ease of use and navigation).<br />
We have 25% of clients logging in and we’d like more.<br />
Not a big value proposition currently - we’d like more value for Recipients, when they log in. We’d like them to self serve more and fewer calls to staff.<br />
200 users a day, mainly clicking on invoices and transactions.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Audience</p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Recipients, Care Partners, registered supporters,</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Outcome</p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>The Recipient Uplift initiative will redesign and enhance the recipient-facing experience in the Trilogy Care portal. It aims to make navigation simpler, surface key package information at a glance, deliver a more self-service, and also be a mobile-optimised interface.</p></td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
