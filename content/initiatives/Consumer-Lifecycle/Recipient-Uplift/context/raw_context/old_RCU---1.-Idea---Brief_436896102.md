&#10;<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Problem Statement</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Currently, the recipient portal is not optimised as a user-friendly website. For example, when a recipient logs in, their data is contained on one long, scrollable page and the most important package information is not immediately available at a glance.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Possible Solution</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>The Recipient Uplift initiative will redesign and enhance the recipient-facing experience in the Trilogy Care portal. It aims to make navigation simpler, surface key package information at a glance, deliver more self-service, and also be a mobile-optimised interface.<br />
Changes will integrate new SAH requirements (eg - <a href="https://trilogycare.atlassian.net/wiki/x/H4AKGg" data-card-appearance="inline" rel="nofollow">https://trilogycare.atlassian.net/wiki/x/H4AKGg</a> digital Home Care Agreement signing) improve clarity around budgets and bills, and ensure recipients can act quickly on important items without needing to contact support.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Benefits</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>For Recipients:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><p><strong>Improved Usability:</strong> Simplified menu structure and intuitive navigation for all device sizes, especially mobile (~40% of use).</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Faster Access to Key Actions:</strong> Dashboard “window” to essential pages with direct links to tasks such as viewing budgets, signing agreements, and managing bills.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Increased Self-Service:</strong> Profile updates, invoice comments, budget requests, and access to unified bills from one place.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Greater Transparency:</strong> Clearer visibility of pending payments, care activity, and package utilisation.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>For Trilogy Care:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><p><strong>Higher Engagement &amp; Retention:</strong> More frequent logins and self-resolution of tasks reduce churn and frustration.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Operational Efficiency:</strong> Fewer support calls for routine queries like “who to contact,” payment status, or navigation help.</p></li>
<li><p><strong>Alignment with SAH Compliance:</strong> Seamless integration of Home Care Agreement signing and other SAH-mandated recipient interactions.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Owner</strong></p></td>

<li><p><strong>External:</strong> Recipients, Representatives/Supporters, Service Providers (indirectly via reduced escalation)</p></li>
</ol></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Assumptions, Dependencies &amp; Risks</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Assumptions:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><p>SAH requirements for recipient interactions (e.g., agreement signing, contribution display) are finalised and can be integrated.</p></li>
<li><p>Core portal infrastructure supports responsive design changes without major re-platforming.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Dependencies:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Completion of Supplier Onboarding and Budget V2 features.</p></li>
<li><p>Updated contact data accuracy to ensure communications and agreements reach the correct recipient/representative.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Risks</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><ul>
<li><p>Design complexity could push development timelines past SAH go-live.</p></li>
<li><p>Significant UX changes may require additional recipient onboarding/training to avoid confusion.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Estimated Effort, Time</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Effort:</strong> 3/5 – Requires design, backend integrations, and frontend rebuild of multiple recipient views.<br />
<strong>Cost:</strong> 2/5 – Moderate, mostly internal design/dev resourcing.<br />
<strong>Time:</strong> ~2 sprints (plus UX design phase).<br />
<strong>Benefit:</strong> 5/5 – High potential impact on satisfaction, engagement, and operational efficiency.</p></td>
</tr>
