# 1. Idea Brief – My Agreements Tab

<div class="table-wrap">

<table class="confluenceTable" data-table-width="1011" data-layout="center" data-local-id="519b1df8-c9c0-4f60-8bab-84258b13b493">
<tbody>
<tr>
<th class="confluenceTh"><p>Section</p></th>
<th class="confluenceTh"><p>Details</p></th>
</tr>
&#10;<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Problem Statement (What)</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>HOME CARE AGREEMENTS (HCA) are currently fragmented across PDFs, email, and manual processes. This causes:</p>
<p>• Delays in onboarding (Home Care Agreements not signed promptly).</p>
<p>• Lack of visibility into agreement status for clients and staff.</p>
<p>• Compliance risks due to missing/inconsistent consent records.</p>
<p>• Inefficiency when management type or coordinator changes trigger fee or responsibility updates.</p>
<p>• No structured way to manage termination/reactivation of agreements.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Possible Solution (How)</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Introduce an <strong>My Agreements tab</strong> in Portal with:</p>
<p>• <strong>List View</strong>: Agreements displayed with Type, Status, Created Date, Signed Date.</p>
<p>• <strong>Detail View</strong>: Inline viewer, metadata, signature object, and audit history.• <strong>First Login Flow</strong>: New clients routed to HCA signing.</p>
<p>• <strong>Change Option Workflow</strong>: Generates Amendments when switching Self-Managed ↔︎ Self-Management Plus.</p>
<p>• <strong>Coordinator Change Workflow</strong>: Generates Amendments with updated coordinator details and fee loadings, requiring client signature.</p>
<p>• <strong>Termination/Reactivation</strong>: Agreements can be formally terminated or reactivated with audit tracking.</p>
<p>• <strong>Consent Variation Table</strong>: Governs use of digital, scanned, and voice-transcript consent methods.</p>
<p>• <strong>Basic Facts Panel</strong>: Shows management option, fee basis, effective date, last amendment date.</p>
<p>• <strong>Staff Tools</strong>: Initiate agreements, send for signature, upload signed artefacts.•</p>
<p><strong>Notifications</strong>: Clients alerted when agreements are Sent, pending, Signed, or Terminated.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Benefits (Why)</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><ul>
<li><p>Faster onboarding: ≥85% of HCAs signed within 48h.</p></li>
<li><p>• Stronger compliance: 100% agreements logged with consent method and artefact.</p></li>
<li><p>• Reduced operational load: −30% emails for chasing agreements.</p></li>
<li><p>• Clear client empowerment: transparency on agreements and fees.</p></li>
<li><p>• Stable commercial controls: all management and coordinator changes backed by signed amendments.</p></li>
<li><p>• Improved audit readiness: full version history, including Signed, Terminated, Reactivated.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Owner (Who)</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>Head of Product Design</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Other Stakeholders</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Accountable</strong>: Product, Engineering, Compliance/Legal</p>
<p><strong>Consulted</strong>: Care Partners, Client Services, Operations</p>
<p><strong>Informed</strong>: Sales, Marketing, Training</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Assumptions &amp; Dependencies, Risks</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Assumptions</strong>: Digital signing available; notifications service integrated; RBAC enforced.</p>
<p><strong>Dependencies</strong>: Agreement templates maintained by Compliance; coordinator change data from internal systems.</p>
<p><strong>Risks</strong>: Compliance gaps if consent variation rules not enforced; risk of client frustration if signing flows fail; misuse of manual consent capture if not auditable.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p><strong>Estimated Effort</strong></p></td>
<td class="confluenceTd"><p>~4 sprints (~$360k equivalent at $90k per sprint), covering list/detail views, workflows, coordinator changes, terminations/reactivations, staff tools, audit trail, and notifications.</p></td>
</tr>
