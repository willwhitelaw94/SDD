**The Story**: Mary just got approved for home care. She receives an email with a magic link.

Soft blue and cream colors. Friendly cartoon style.` |

## Image Generation Prompts

Use these with DALL-E, Midjourney, or similar:

### Friendly Style
```
Simple, warm illustration style. Soft colors (blue, green, cream).
Rounded shapes. Diverse elderly characters. Modern devices (tablets, phones).
Healthcare/home care setting. Accessibility-friendly design.
No text in images. Clean backgrounds.
```

### Example Prompts

**US01 Panel 1:**
> Warm illustration of an elderly woman with gray hair sitting at a kitchen table,
> looking at a laptop with a pleasant expression. Email notification icon floating
> above. Soft morning light. Simple, friendly style. No text.

**US02 Panel 4:**
> Simple illustration of a middle-aged man's hand drawing a signature on a
> smartphone screen with his finger. Clean white background. Warm, approachable style.

**US05 Panel 1:**
> Friendly illustration of a female healthcare coordinator in casual professional
> clothes sitting with an elderly woman in a cozy living room. Tablet visible.
> Warm, supportive atmosphere. Simple cartoon style.

## Usage

1. **Designer handoff** - Share this doc with designer to create illustrations
2. **AI generation** - Use prompts directly with image AI tools
3. **Stakeholder communication** - Walk through storyboards in meetings
4. **User testing** - Validate flows before building UI
5. **Documentation** - Include in PRD or confluence pages
