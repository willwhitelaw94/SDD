Overview

Lead Essential establishes the native lead management module in the Portal — replacing Zoho CRM as the primary workspace for viewing, managing, and progressing leads through the sales funnel.

Key Actors: Sales Staff, Sales Team Leader / Head of Sales, Lead Owner (consumer/representative)

Epic Dependencies: LTH (depends on), LGI (blocks), LDS (blocks)

US1: Staff Leads List View
As a Sales Staff member — P1 Critical
Why This Priority

The list view is the primary workspace for sales staff. Without it, there is no central place to manage leads. The conversion dashboard (already built) provides a starting point, but LES requires additional columns and capabilities beyond conversion tracking.

Dependencies
US1: List
→
US9: Creation
Acceptance Scenarios (5)

Scenario 1

Given I am a sales staff member, When I navigate to the Leads page, Then I see a table displaying: Lead Name, Contact Name, Email, Phone, Journey Stage, Lead Status, Assigned Agent, Created Date, and Conversion status.

Scenario 2

Given I am viewing the leads list, When I apply filters for Journey Stage, Lead Status, Assigned Agent, or Conversion status, Then the list updates to show only matching leads.

Scenario 3

Given I am viewing the leads list, When I click on a lead name, Then I am navigated to that lead's profile view.

Scenario 4

Given I am viewing the leads list, When I click "Create Lead", Then a lead creation form appears with mandatory fields (Lead Name, Email) and optional fields, and upon save the new lead appears in the list.

Scenario 5

Given I am a Sales Team Leader, When I select multiple leads using checkboxes, Then I can perform bulk actions (assign agent, export).

US4: Journey Stage Tracking
As a Sales Staff member — P1 Critical
Why This Priority

Journey Stage is the core sales pipeline metric. Every reporting dashboard, forecast, and SLA depends on accurate stage tracking. Without it, there is no funnel visibility.

Dependencies

Acceptance Scenarios (5)

Scenario 1

Given I am viewing a lead's profile, When I look at the Journey Stage indicator, Then I see the lead's current position along the funnel: Pre-Care → ACAT Booked → Approved → Allocated → Active → Converted, with the current stage highlighted.

Scenario 2

Given I click to update the Journey Stage, When the wizard opens, Then I can select any stage (forward or backward) and see mandatory fields that must be completed for the target stage.

Acceptance Scenarios (5)

Scenario 1

Given I am viewing a lead's profile, When I see the Lead Status actions, Then I can select from: Not Contacted, Attempted Contact, Made Contact, and Lost.

Scenario 2 — Made Contact

Given I update status to "Made Contact", When the wizard opens, Then I see mandatory fields: Journey Stage, Lead Attribution, Purchase Intent; optional: contact method, outcome, follow-up date, notes.

Scenario 3 — Attempted Contact

Given I update status to "Attempted Contact", When the wizard opens, Then I see mandatory: Contact Method, Contact Outcome (No Answer, Left Voicemail, Line Busy, Wrong Number, Email Bounced, Other); optional: notes.

Scenario 4 — Lost

Given I update status to "Lost", When the wizard opens, Then I see mandatory: Lost Type + dynamic Reason dropdown. Lost Lead (Chose competitor, Too expensive, Not ready, Changed mind, Other), Junk (Spam, Test entry, Duplicate, Invalid data), Uncontactable (No response after 5 attempts, Wrong number, Deceased, Moved overseas).

Scenario 5

Given any status update is saved, When I view the timeline, Then I see a chronological entry with old status, new status, timestamp, agent name, and any notes captured.

US9: Internal Lead Creation
As a Sales Staff member — P2 Important
Why This Priority

Staff need to capture leads in real-time during calls. A quick create path (modal) for minimal capture and a guided stepper for detailed intake gives flexibility without friction.

Acceptance Scenarios (5)

Scenario 1

Given I am on the leads list view, When I click "Create Lead", Then I see two options: "Quick Create" (modal) and "Guided Create" (stepper).

Scenario 2 — Quick Create

Given I choose "Quick Create", When the modal opens, Then I see mandatory: Lead Name, Email; optional: phone, relationship, notes. On save, lead is created and I remain on the list view.

Scenario 3 — Guided Create

Given I choose "Guided Create", When the stepper opens, Then I'm walked through 3 steps: Personal Details, Aged Care Stage, Contact Information. On completion, navigated to new lead's profile.

Scenario 4

Given I fill mandatory fields via either path, When the lead is created, Then it appears in the list with me as assigned agent, default Journey Stage "Pre-Care", default Lead Status "Not Contacted".

Scenario 5

Given a lead with the same email exists, When I attempt to save, Then the system warns of potential duplicate and allows me to proceed or navigate to existing lead.

Edge Cases
6 edge cases identified in the spec

Duplicate email on create

System warns staff of potential duplicate. Staff can proceed (different person, same email) or navigate to existing lead.

Staff edits unassigned lead

All staff with view-all-leads permission can view and edit any lead. Assignment is for accountability, not access control.

Section validation errors

Form shows inline validation errors. Section not saved until all required fields within that section are valid.

Legacy leads from old wizard

Appear in staff list with whatever data they have. Missing sections show as incomplete in profile completion indicator.

Lost lead re-engagement

Staff can change status back from "Lost" to "Not Contacted" or "Attempted Contact" with a required note explaining re-engagement.

Zoho sync data conflict

Portal is source of truth for fields edited in Portal. Zoho-synced fields manually overridden in Portal are not overwritten by subsequent syncs.
