================================================================================
       STEP 3: QUESTIONNAIRE — LAYOUT VARIATIONS (6 OPTIONS)
================================================================================

Step 3 captures client details, funding/financial info, and meeting booking.
Three sub-sections:
  - Client Details (Gender, DOB, Email, Phone, Country, Nationality, Language,
    Interpreter Required)
  - Funding & Financial (Funding Status, Pensioner Status, Contribution Rates,
    Default to max, Payment Method)
  - Scheduling (Calendly booking, Meeting Date/Time)

Note: Commencement/Cessation/Closing dates were captured in Step 1 but
Commencement defaults to Meeting Date when booked via Calendly.

================================================================================
RECOMMENDATION
================================================================================

**Option D (Progressive with Conditional Funding)** recommended because:
- FSO/MSO as radio buttons makes the binary choice obvious
- Inline explanation of funding status reduces errors
- "Default to max" checkbox disabling rate fields prevents confusion
- Payment method as radio shows all 4 options at once
- Clear Calendly section with helpful messaging

**Option B (Card-Based)** is best if using Card-Based accordion (Option G overall)
for visual consistency — cards within cards.

**Option E (Calendly States)** is essential as a supplement — use its booked state
and fallback patterns regardless of which main layout is chosen.

**Option C (Two-Column)** is best for power users who want minimal scrolling.
