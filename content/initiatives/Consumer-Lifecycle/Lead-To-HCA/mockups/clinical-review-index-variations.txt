================================================================================
       CLINICAL REVIEW INDEX — LAYOUT VARIATIONS (6 OPTIONS)
================================================================================

The Clinical Review Index is a dedicated page under Consumers where Clinical
Nurses see clients flagged for review (~2% of conversions).

Key requirements:
  - Show pending reviews with SLA countdown (24hr target)
  - Allow recording Clinical Screening Outcome
  - Three outcomes: Cleared for Preferred, SM+ Required, Not Suitable
  - Notify Sales when review is complete (FR-035)
  - Show SLA status (FR-038)

================================================================================
RECOMMENDATION
================================================================================

**Option C (Split Panel)** recommended because:
- Clinical nurses can select a client and record outcome without page navigation
- SLA visible in both list and detail
- Risk flags and assessment context visible before making clinical decision
- Mirrors CommonSplitPanel pattern already used in Portal
- Works well for the expected volume (~2-3 reviews at any time)

**Option D (Queue/Inbox)** is best if urgent/standard grouping adds value.

**Option A (Data Table)** is the safe fallback — familiar, sortable, scales well
if volume unexpectedly grows.
