================================================================================

================================================================================

  A. Full Agreement (Suitable or clinically cleared) — signature capture
  B. Sample Agreement (Needs Clinical Attention) — no signature, send sample

Signature types: Verbal (audio upload), Digital (email), Manual (PDF)
