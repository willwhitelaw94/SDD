================================================================================
RECOMMENDATION
================================================================================

**Option E (Progressive Disclosure)** recommended because:
- Classification-dependent date fields appear contextually (less confusion)
- Radio buttons for Management Option make the 3 choices obvious
- Representative shown as cards allows for future multi-rep support
- Opt-out consent is clean and inline
- Balances information density with clarity

**Option A (Two-Column)** is the safe fallback — conventional, everything visible.

**Option F (Compact)** best for power users who want minimal scrolling.
