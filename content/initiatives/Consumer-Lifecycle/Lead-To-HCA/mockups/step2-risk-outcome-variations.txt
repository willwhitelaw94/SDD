================================================================================
         STEP 2: RISK ASSESSMENT OUTCOME — LAYOUT VARIATIONS (6 OPTIONS)
================================================================================

Step 2 receives the Risk Score Outcome from the Assessment Tool (external).
This step has 4 states:
  - Waiting (Assessment Tool not yet completed)
  - Suitable for Everything (~98%)
  - Needs Clinical Attention (~2%)
  - Not Suited (rare — process stops)

The salesperson cannot proceed past Step 2 until the outcome is received.

confusion about what "Needs Clinical Attention" means for the salesperson.
